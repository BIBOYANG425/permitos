## Triggers

- Stationary source emits or uses substances on the program's Appendix A list.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AB 2588 reporting | Inclusion is set by the LOCAL air district (no single statewide tonnage trigger) | 17 CCR 93300 et seq.; HSC 44300 et seq. |

## Exemptions

- The reporting universe and prioritization criteria are district-specific; confirm the facility's air district's inclusion rule.
