# VCAPCD Rule 10 — Permit Conditions

## Authority to Construct Conditions (Section A.3)

The APCO is required to include in every ATC:

1. **Description** of the emissions units for which the ATC is issued.

2. **Compliance conditions** — "Any reasonable conditions determined by the APCO pursuant to Rule 29 to be necessary to assure or demonstrate that the stationary source will operate in compliance with applicable federal, state and local rules and regulations."
   - Conditions may include, but are not limited to:
     - Requirements to perform source testing.
     - Requirements to apply for a Permit to Operate.
     - Requirements to obtain emission reduction credits.

3. **Disclaimer statement** — "A statement that the granting of the Authority to Construct shall not be construed as an endorsement by the APCD or guarantee compliance with APCD Rules and Regulations."

## Permit to Operate Conditions (Section B.2)

The APCO is required to include in every PTO:

1. **Description** of all emissions units at the stationary source covered by the permit.

2. **Compliance conditions** — "Any reasonable conditions determined by the APCO pursuant to Rule 29 to be necessary to assure or demonstrate that a stationary source and all emissions units at the stationary source will operate in compliance with all applicable federal, state and local emission standards."

3. **Permitted Emissions** — "The amount of emissions that the APCO determines a stationary source may emit calculated pursuant to Rule 29. This amount is called 'Permitted Emissions.'"

4. **Disclaimer statement** — "A statement that the Permit to Operate shall not be construed to allow any emissions unit to operate in violation of any applicable State or Federal emission standard or rules of the District."

## Temporary Permit to Operate Conditions (Section B.4)

A Temporary PTO:
- Specifies conditions under which the emissions units may be operated during the processing of a full PTO application.
- Expires automatically when a PTO is issued or when the PTO application is denied.

## Key Cross-References

- **Rule 29** governs the APCO's authority to set permit conditions and the method for calculating Permitted Emissions.
- **Rule 30** governs annual renewal of Permits to Operate.
- **Rule 11** defines "small" and "medium" stationary source classifications used in the ATC exemption.
- **Rule 23** lists equipment categories exempt from all permit requirements.
