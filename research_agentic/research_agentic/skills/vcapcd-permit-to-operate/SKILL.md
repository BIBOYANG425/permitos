## Triggers

- Constructing, operating, or modifying an emissions unit at a stationary source in Ventura County.
- New or modified equipment (printing, coating, spray, combustion, solvent cleaning) that can emit air contaminants (ROC/VOC, PM, NOx, etc.).

## Determination path

| Step | Expected path | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Permit trigger | Rule 10 requires Authority to Construct + Permit to Operate | VCAPCD Rule 10 |
| Exemption test | unless exempt under Rule 23 | VCAPCD Rule 23 ([[vcapcd-rule-23-exemption]]) |
| Substantive rule | graphic arts / coating / solvent rules (74-series) | VCAPCD Rule 74.x ([[vcapcd-rule-74-graphic-arts]]) |

## Exemptions

- Equipment that meets a Rule 23 exemption does not require a Rule 10 permit — confirm the specific exemption and its conditions/recordkeeping in the fetched Rule 23.
