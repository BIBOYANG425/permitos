The agent must still fetch the cited primary source and quote it verbatim: the skill ORIENTS, the fetched regulation is the PROOF. Never cite a SKILL.md as evidence.

## Triggers

- Construction (clearing, grading, excavation) that disturbs soil.
- A site that is part of a larger common plan of development even if its own footprint is small.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CGP coverage required | Construction disturbs one or more acres of soil | California Construction General Permit |

## Exemptions

- Disturbance below one acre AND not part of a larger common plan of development generally does not trigger the CGP — confirm both conditions against the fetched permit; the common-plan rule is the common trap.
