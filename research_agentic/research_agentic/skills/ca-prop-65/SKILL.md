The agent must still fetch the cited primary source and quote it verbatim: the skill ORIENTS, the fetched regulation is the PROOF. Never cite a SKILL.md as evidence.

## Triggers

- Business knowingly exposes individuals to, or discharges into water, a chemical on the OEHHA Prop 65 list.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Warning required | Exposure above the chemical's safe-harbor level (NSRL / MADL) | 27 CCR 25600 et seq.; HSC 25249.6 |

## Exemptions

- Businesses with fewer than 10 employees are exempt. Exposure below the safe-harbor level needs no warning.
