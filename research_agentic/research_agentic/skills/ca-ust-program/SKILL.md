## Triggers

- A tank (plus connected piping) stores a hazardous substance substantially or totally beneath ground.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Regulated UST | Any qualifying underground tank is regulated (no general gallon floor) | HSC 25281 / 23 CCR Ch.16 |

## Exemptions

- Farm motor-fuel tanks <=1,100 gal (non-resale) and residential heating-oil tanks <=1,100 gal are excluded (HSC 25281(y)).
