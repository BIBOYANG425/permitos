## Triggers

- Facility accumulates batteries, lamps, electronic devices, CRTs, PV modules, aerosols, or mercury devices.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Universal-waste handler standards | Accumulating universal waste -> streamlined handler standards instead of full Subtitle C | 22 CCR 66273.1 et seq. |

## Exemptions

- Universal waste is still hazardous; it is managed under streamlined standards, not exempt from regulation.
