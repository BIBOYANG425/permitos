## Triggers

- Facility's potential to emit may reach major-source thresholds for a regulated pollutant or HAP.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Title V major source | PTE >= 100 tpy of a regulated pollutant (lower in nonattainment); 10 tpy single / 25 tpy aggregate HAP | 40 CFR 70; HSC 42300 et seq.; district rules |

## Exemptions

- Nonattainment-area thresholds are lower and area/pollutant-specific. A federally enforceable PTE limit can keep a source minor.
