description: Ventura County APCD Rule 23 — exemptions from the Rule 10 permit. Graphic-arts operations (including ink-jet printing) are exempt where less than 200 pounds each of ROC, methylene chloride, 1,1,1-trichloroethane, and perchloroethylene are lost to the atmosphere during every rolling 12-consecutive-calendar-month period (emissions counted from inks, cleaning, coatings, and adhesives). Exemptions require usage/emission records to substantiate.

## Triggers

- An emitting operation seeking exemption from the Rule 10 permit.
- Graphic arts / printing (incl. ink-jet) operations — Rule 23(F)(13).

## Thresholds

| Operation | Expected exemption threshold | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Graphic arts / ink-jet printing | < 200 lb ROC lost per rolling 12 months (and < 200 lb each of the listed chlorinated solvents) | VCAPCD Rule 23(F)(13) |

## Exemptions / conditions

- The exemption requires records (usage, emissions, calculations) to substantiate that the threshold is not exceeded — an exemption claimed without records is not supportable.
- Emissions are aggregated across inks, ink additives, application-equipment cleaning, coatings, and adhesives associated with the operation — confirm the aggregation rule in the fetched text.
- Acetone and other exempt compounds are generally not ROC; isopropyl alcohol is ROC — verify each compound's ROC status.
