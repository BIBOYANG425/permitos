## Annual Operating Emissions Fee (in addition to renewal fee)

Applies when facility emits ≥4 TPY of VOC, NOx, SOx, or particulate matter (or ≥100 TPY CO; >0.1 TPY ammonia) from permitted equipment. Flat base emissions fee: **$176.41/year**.

Late filing surcharges:
- <30 days late: 5% of reported amount
- 30–90 days late: 15%
- 91 days to 1 year: 25%

## Streamlined Standard Permit Processing Fee

For qualifying equipment types: **$1,334.82** flat (no small business discount on this base). Standard Streamlined Permits apply to common off-the-shelf equipment types.

## Processing Timeline

Rule 301 does not set statutory processing deadlines for routine permit applications. The five-step review (screening → completeness → compliance evaluation → approval/denial → issuance) is administered by SCAQMD Permit Services:

- Fees are due at time of filing (or upon notification for certain fees, due within 30 days).
- Applications deemed incomplete are suspended until completion; processing clock restarts on completeness.
- Once a PC expires (Rule 205 provisions), the applicant may re-apply at the administrative change fee rate if the rules and BACT have not changed, provided reapplication is within **1 year of expiration**.

## Solar Energy Fee Credit

Permittees with solar energy equipment on-site may receive an annual fee credit of **$2.60 per 1,000 Btu/hr** of solar design capacity (credit capped at total annual operating permit renewal fees at the site).
