## Triggers

- Equipment that appears on the Rule 219 exempt-equipment list.
- A project seeking to avoid a Permit to Construct by qualifying for a listed exemption.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rule 219 exemption available | Equipment matches a listed category AND all listed conditions are satisfied | SCAQMD Rule 219 |

## Exemptions

- An exemption applies ONLY when every listed condition for that equipment is met. Do not assume an exemption; verify each condition against the fetched rule text. A single failed condition pushes the equipment back to the Rule 201 Permit to Construct requirement.
