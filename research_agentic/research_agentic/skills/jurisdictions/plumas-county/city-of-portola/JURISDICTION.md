> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Portola** (Plumas County's only incorporated city). ## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Plumas County Public Health Agency, Environmental Health Division (countywide CUPA). HMBP, UST, APSA, HWG, CalARP, and CERS reporting for Portola facilities are administered by the county. Office at 520 Main Street, Room 309, Quincy, CA 95971; (530) 283-6355.
- https://plumascounty.us/276/Hazardous-Materials-Management-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- The City of Portola does **not** run a municipal fire department. As of July 1, 2024, fire service is provided by the Beckwourth Peak Fire Protection District (a consolidated district that absorbed the City of Portola, former Sierra Valley FPD, Gold Mountain CSD, and Beckwourth FPD; Eastern Plumas Rural FPD annexed in December 2024). District: (530) 832-1008. State Responsibility Area wildland protection: CAL FIRE Lassen-Modoc Unit.
- https://www.cityofportola.gov/fire
- https://bpfpd.ca.gov/history

## Building authority

- City of Portola Building Department — issues building permits for new construction, additions, renovations, and other permitted work within the city. Run under the Department of Public Works (Director of Public Works Todd Roberts; Building Inspector Dave Mann). Portola City Hall, 35 Third Ave, Portola, CA 96122; (530) 832-6809.
- https://www.cityofportola.gov/building-department
