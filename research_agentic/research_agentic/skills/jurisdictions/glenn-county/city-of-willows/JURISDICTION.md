> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Willows** (Glenn County seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Glenn County Agriculture / Weights & Measures Department (countywide CUPA, HMBP administered including incorporated cities). https://www.countyofglenn.net/government/departments/agriculture-weights-measures/cupa-certified-unified-program-agency

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Willows Fire Department (municipal) — serves the City of Willows and the Willows Rural Fire Protection District; provides fire prevention/inspection, weed abatement, and Hazardous Material Team response. https://www.cityofwillows.org/dept/fire

## Building authority

- City of Willows — Building (Title 15 of the Willows Municipal Code). https://www.codepublishing.com/CA/Willows/

## Notes

- Willows is the Glenn County seat and runs its own municipal fire department with a HazMat Team response capability, but HazMat **regulatory** (CUPA) authority remains with the county Ag/W&M Department — the city fire HazMat team is emergency-response, not the CUPA.
