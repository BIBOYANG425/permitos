## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Glenn County Agriculture / Weights & Measures Department — CUPA for the **entire county including the incorporated cities of Orland and Willows** (administers HMBP/Area Plan, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA aboveground petroleum, and CalARP). NOTE: CalARP/RMP submittals are directed to the Glenn County Air Pollution Control District.
- https://www.countyofglenn.net/government/departments/agriculture-weights-measures/cupa-certified-unified-program-agency

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Unincorporated areas: multiple independent fire protection districts (e.g., Orland FPD, Ord FPD, Glenn-Codora FPD) with CAL FIRE wildland support. https://www.countyofglenn.net/government/committees-commissions/orland-fire-protection-district-glenn-county
- City of Willows: Willows Fire Department (municipal). https://www.cityofwillows.org/dept/fire
- City of Orland: served by the Orland Volunteer Fire Department / Orland (Rural) Fire Protection District partnership (city hired a shared paid Fire Chief in 2019; not an independent city-run department). https://www.cityoforland.com/fire-department/

## Building authority

- Glenn County Planning & Community Development Services Agency (PCDSA), Building Division (unincorporated). https://www.countyofglenn.net/government/departments/planning-community-development-services/building

## Notes

- GOTCHA: The CUPA is housed in the **Agriculture / Weights & Measures Department**, not a county Environmental Health division (Environmental Health sits under PCDSA). Heavily agricultural caseload — farms with aboveground petroleum and pesticide/chemical storage are the dominant facility type.
- GOTCHA: **CalARP / RMP** submittals go to the Glenn County **Air Pollution Control District**, a separate body from the CUPA proper. https://www.countyofglenn.net/dept/agriculture/cupa-certified-unified-program-agency/calarp-risk-management-plans
- Whether any city operates as a CUPA Participating Agency (PA) vs. fully county-run is UNVERIFIED; HMBP program is stated to be administered countywide including the incorporated cities.
