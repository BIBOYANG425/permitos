## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Glenn County Agriculture / Weights & Measures Department (countywide CUPA, HMBP administered including incorporated cities). https://www.countyofglenn.net/government/departments/agriculture-weights-measures/cupa-certified-unified-program-agency

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Orland fire service is provided through a partnership of the City of Orland, the Orland (Rural) Fire Protection District, and the Orland Volunteer Fire Department (OVFD); the city/district hired a shared paid Fire Chief in 2019. Not an independent city-run department. https://www.cityoforland.com/fire-department/
- Fire prevention/inspection of businesses and multi-family buildings: Orland Fire Protection District. https://www.orlandfire.org/inspections-and-permits

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Orland does **not** operate a standalone municipal fire department — service is a city / fire-protection-district / volunteer-department partnership sharing one paid Fire Chief. HazMat regulatory (CUPA) authority remains with the county Ag/W&M Department.
