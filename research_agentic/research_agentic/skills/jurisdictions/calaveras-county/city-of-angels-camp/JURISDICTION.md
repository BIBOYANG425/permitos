> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Angels Camp** (the only incorporated city in Calaveras County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs inside the city limits and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Calaveras County Environmental Management Agency, Environmental Health Department (countywide CUPA, certified 2002-06-01).
- https://ema.calaverasgov.us/Environmental-Health/CUPA-Hazardous-Materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Angels Fire Department — its OWN municipal (combination full-time/part-time/volunteer) department, two stations, ISO rating 4 — https://angelscamp.gov/fire/

## Building authority

- City of Angels Camp Building Department, Community & Economic Development (200 Monte Verde St.) — https://angelscamp.gov/community-development/building-department/

## Notes

- Angels Camp is the only incorporated city in Calaveras County; everything else is unincorporated and falls to the county.
- Despite running its own fire department, the city is NOT a CUPA and is NOT a Participating Agency — hazmat/HMBP/UST/etc. inside city limits are administered by the county CUPA.
