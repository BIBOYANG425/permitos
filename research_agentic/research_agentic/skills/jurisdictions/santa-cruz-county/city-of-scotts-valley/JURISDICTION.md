> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Scotts Valley** (Santa Cruz County, San Lorenzo Valley gateway). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the County of Santa Cruz Health Services Agency, Environmental Health Services (EHS), the single county-wide CUPA. HMBP / hazwaste generator / UST / APSA / CalARP for facilities inside Scotts Valley are administered by the county EHS CUPA.
- https://scceh.com/NewHome/Programs/HazardousMaterialsWaste.aspx

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Scotts Valley Fire Protection District — independent special district (not a city department) providing fire and EMS to the City of Scotts Valley and surrounding area — https://www.scottsvalleyfire.com/

## Building authority

- City of Scotts Valley Building Department (permits/inspection within city limits; not the county Unified Permit Center) — https://www.scottsvalley.gov/208/Building-Department

## Notes

- GOTCHA: fire protection for Scotts Valley is provided by the independent Scotts Valley Fire Protection District, NOT a municipal city fire department and NOT the CAL FIRE-contracted county fire department. The city does run its own building department. Hazmat/Unified Program permitting routes to Santa Cruz County EHS.
