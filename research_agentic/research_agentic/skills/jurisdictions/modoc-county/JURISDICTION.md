## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Modoc County Environmental Health serves as the single Certified Unified Program Agency (CUPA) for the entire county, administering the Unified Program elements (HMBP/Hazardous Materials Business Plan, hazardous waste generator / used oil, UST, APSA aboveground storage tanks; CalARP not explicitly listed on the page — UNVERIFIED). No participating-agency split listed. Office at 202 W. 4th St., Alturas, CA 96101; phone 530-233-6310. The lone incorporated city (Alturas) is served by the county CUPA.
- https://environmentalhealth.co.modoc.ca.us/nav/certified_unified_program_agency_(cupa).php
- CalEPA Unified Program Regulator Directory (search "Modoc"): https://cersapps.calepa.ca.gov/Public/Directory

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- No single countywide structural fire department. Unincorporated communities are served by independent fire protection districts / volunteer departments (e.g., Alturas Rural FPD, California Pines / Cal Pines Volunteer FD via the California Pines Community Services District). The City of Alturas runs its own municipal fire department (see city file). State Responsibility Area (SRA) wildland protection falls under CAL FIRE Lassen-Modoc Unit (covers Lassen, Modoc, Plumas and portions of Shasta/Siskiyou); the Modoc National Forest (USFS) handles federal-land wildfire.
- https://www.fire.ca.gov/ (CAL FIRE) — Lassen-Modoc Unit
- https://cpcsd.specialdistrict.org/cal-pines-fire-department (California Pines / Cal Pines FD)

## Building authority

- Modoc County Building and Safety Department (Modoc County Building Department) — building permits, plan checks, inspections for the unincorporated area; applies Title 24 uniform building standards. Office at 203 West 4th St., Alturas, CA 96101; phone 530-233-6406.
- https://www.co.modoc.ca.us/departments/building_and_safety/index.php

## Local code amendments

- Modoc County Code Title 15 (Buildings and Regulations) adopts the California Building Standards Code (Title 24) — published on Municode. The county Building and Safety page states new structures are regulated under "the adopted building Code(s), Title 24 of CCR" but does not state the specific adopted edition. The adopted Title 24 *edition* (2022 vs. 2025) is UNVERIFIED — confirm whether the county has formally adopted the 2025 edition that took effect statewide 2026-01-01.
- https://library.municode.com/ca/modoc_county/codes/code_of_ordinances?nodeId=TIT15BURE

## Notes

- GOTCHA (single incorporated city): Alturas is the **only** incorporated city in Modoc County (county seat). Every other community (California Pines, Cedarville, Newell, Adin, Canby, etc.) is unincorporated and falls under county authority for CUPA and building, with fire service fragmented across local fire protection districts / volunteer departments and CAL FIRE SRA coverage.
- Large federal- and tribal-land footprint (Modoc National Forest / USFS, BLM, plus tribal land such as the Alturas Indian Rancheria) — expect federal- and tribal-land carve-outs where USFS/BLM or the tribe, not the county, is the land manager/authority.
- The county CUPA, Environmental Health, Building and Safety, and Planning departments are all clustered on W. 4th St. in Alturas (CUPA at 202, Building at 203).
