## CUPA (Unified Program)

- San Bernardino County Fire Protection District, Hazardous Materials Division — CUPA for the whole county **except the City of Victorville**
- https://sbcfire.org/hazmatcupa/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- San Bernardino County Fire Protection District — covers ~86% of the county after absorbing many city/district departments — https://sbcfire.org

## Building authority

- San Bernardino County Land Use Services — Building & Safety. Title 24 with county amendments; specific URL UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA (CUPA split): Victorville runs its own CUPA (Victorville Fire Dept); County Fire is CUPA everywhere else.
- GOTCHA (consolidation): County Fire absorbed Adelanto (1999), Hesperia FPD (2004), Victorville FD (2008), and the **City of San Bernardino FD (annexed 2016-07-01)**. Do not list a separate City of San Bernardino FD.
