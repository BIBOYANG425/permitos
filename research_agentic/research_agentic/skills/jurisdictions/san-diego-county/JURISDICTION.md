## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (SDAPCD)
- **San Diego Air Pollution Control District (SDAPCD)** controls all air permits in San Diego County
- Permits: https://www.sdapcd.org | (858) 586-2600
- Key rules: Rule 10 (permit), Rule 11 (exemptions similar to SCAQMD Rule 219)

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA
- **San Diego County DEH** (Department of Environmental Health) is the CUPA for unincorporated areas
- City of San Diego has own CUPA (San Diego Fire-Rescue Dept, (619) 533-4300)
- County CUPA: (858) 505-6700 | CERS: https://cers.calepa.ca.gov

### Wastewater
- **Metropolitan Wastewater Department** (City of San Diego) — major POTW
- Industrial Pretreatment Section: (619) 533-4170
- County: various water districts serve unincorporated areas

### Stormwater
- County of San Diego Watershed Protection Program
- MS4 permit covers county and municipalities
- https://www.sandiegocounty.gov/deh/water/watershed.html

### Fire Code
- County of San Diego Fire Authority (unincorporated)
- City of San Diego Fire-Rescue Department (city limits)
- CFC adopted with local amendments

### Zoning
- County: Planning and Development Services, (858) 694-3962
- City of SD: Development Services Department, (619) 446-5000
