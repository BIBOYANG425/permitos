> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Modesto** (county seat and largest city in Stanislaus County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs within city limits and where the city's adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Stanislaus County Department of Environmental Resources (DER), Hazardous Materials Division (the sole countywide CUPA). — https://www.stancounty.com/er/hazmat/cupa.shtm

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Modesto Fire Department — municipal fire department serving the City of Modesto. Also provides administrative/command services to Turlock and fire protection to Ceres under separate contracts. — https://www.modestogov.com/149/Fire-Department

## Building authority

- City of Modesto Building Safety Division (Community & Economic Development). — https://www.modestogov.com/ (verify exact building-division page)

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Modesto Fire Department's reach extends beyond city limits via contracts — it administers/commands the Turlock Fire Department (contract Jan 2022–June 2026) and provides fire protection within the City of Ceres (contract from June 2021). These are operational/administrative arrangements only; hazmat CUPA authority for all of these jurisdictions remains with the county DER.
