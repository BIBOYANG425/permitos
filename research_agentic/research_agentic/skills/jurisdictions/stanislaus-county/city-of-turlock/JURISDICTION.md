> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Turlock** (second-largest city in Stanislaus County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs within city limits and where the city's adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Stanislaus County Department of Environmental Resources (DER), Hazardous Materials Division (the sole countywide CUPA). — https://www.stancounty.com/er/hazmat/cupa.shtm

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Turlock Fire Department — municipal fire department serving the City of Turlock. Since January 2022, administration/management/command services are provided under contract by the Modesto Fire Department (agreement runs Jan 2022 through June 2026); the Turlock Fire Department remains the legal AHJ. — https://www.turlock.ca.us/firedepartment/aboutus/

## Building authority

- City of Turlock Building Division (Development Services). — https://www.cityofturlock.org/ (verify exact building-division page)

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Turlock Fire Department is administratively run by Modesto Fire under a 2022–2026 contract. This is an operational/administrative consolidation, NOT CUPA delegation — hazmat CUPA authority remains with the county DER.
