## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Siskiyou County Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA). HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, and CalARP for Mount Shasta facilities are administered by the County through CERS.
- https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/environmentalhealth/page/hazardous-materials-management-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Mt. Shasta Fire** — a merged operation of Mt. Shasta City Fire and the Mt. Shasta Fire Protection District, combined in 2016 to serve the city and surrounding ~60-square-mile district (~7,000 population). Staffed by a full-time Chief, paid staff, and volunteers. — https://www.mtshastaca.gov/fire-department
- Mt. Shasta Fire Protection District: https://mtshastafiredistrict.gov/
- Surrounding State Responsibility Area wildland: CAL FIRE Siskiyou Unit — https://www.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-protection/cal-fire-units (confirm)
