> Local EHS authority reference for **Siskiyou County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Siskiyou County Environmental Health Division (part of the Community Development Department) — the **sole countywide CUPA**, certified by Cal/EPA effective 1997-01-01 (HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, CalARP). No incorporated city in the county runs its own CUPA or is listed as a separate Participating Agency; all cities (Yreka, Mount Shasta, Weed, Dunsmuir, Montague, Etna, Fort Jones, Dorris, Tulelake) are covered by the County CUPA. Submissions go through CERS.
- https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/environmentalhealth/page/hazardous-materials-management-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Unincorporated / State Responsibility Area: CAL FIRE Siskiyou Unit (Yreka) — https://www.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-protection/cal-fire-units (confirm unit page live)
- Local Responsibility Area within cities and fire-district boundaries is served by municipal fire departments and independent fire protection districts (e.g., Yreka Fire Department, Mt. Shasta Fire, City of Weed Fire Department); see individual city files.
- County emergency services / fire information: https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/emergencyservices/page/fire-updates-and-information

## Building authority

- Siskiyou County Community Development Department, Building Division (unincorporated county; 806 South Main Street, Yreka) — https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/building

## Local code amendments

- Siskiyou County Code of Ordinances is hosted on Municode: https://library.municode.com/ca/siskiyou_county/codes/code_of_ordinances . The County adopted the **2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24)** editions effective **2026-01-01** (Building, Residential, Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing, Energy, WUI, Fire, Existing Building, Green/CALGreen, etc.) — per https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/building/page/current-building-codes

## Notes

- Siskiyou County has nine incorporated cities; **Yreka** (county seat, ~7,600) is the largest, followed by **Mount Shasta** (~3,400), **Weed** (~2,700), and **Dunsmuir** (~1,700). The remainder (Montague, Etna, Fort Jones, Dorris, Tulelake) are small.
- GOTCHA: **Mount Shasta fire is a merged operation** — Mt. Shasta City Fire and the Mt. Shasta Fire Protection District came together in 2016 to jointly serve the city and surrounding district (paid chief + paid staff + volunteers). See the Mount Shasta city file.
- Large amounts of Siskiyou County are **federal land** (Klamath National Forest, Shasta-Trinity National Forest, Lava Beds National Monument); wildland fire on those lands falls to federal agencies (USFS / NPS), not the county or CAL FIRE. Confirm jurisdiction for any specific parcel.
