## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Siskiyou County Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA). HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, and CalARP for Yreka facilities are administered by the County through CERS.
- https://www.siskiyoucounty.gov/environmentalhealth/page/hazardous-materials-management-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Yreka Fire Department** — an all-volunteer municipal department (including the Chief Officer) serving the City of Yreka. — https://www.ci.yreka.ca.us/101/Departments (Fire Department page)
- Surrounding State Responsibility Area wildland: CAL FIRE Siskiyou Unit — https://www.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-protection/cal-fire-units (confirm)

## Building authority

- **City of Yreka Building Department** — examines/approves plans and inspects construction within city limits — https://www.ci.yreka.ca.us/161/Building

## Notes

- Yreka is the **county seat and largest city** in Siskiyou County (~7,600).
- The Yreka Fire Department is all-volunteer; the City does not staff a paid municipal fire department.
