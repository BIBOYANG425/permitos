> Local EHS authority reference for **Orange County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Orange County Health Care Agency, Environmental Health Division — countywide CUPA **except the City of Anaheim**
- https://ochealthinfo.com/about-hca/public-health-services/environmental-health-division/hazardous-materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Orange County Fire Authority (OCFA) — JPA serving unincorporated areas and 23+ cities — https://www.ocfa.org

## Building authority

- OC Public Works — Building & Safety (unincorporated) — https://www.ocpublicworks.com

## Local code amendments

- Adopts CA Building/Fire Codes (Title 24) with local amendments via the Orange County Codified Ordinances. Specific municode URL UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: the County HCA is CUPA everywhere **except Anaheim** (Anaheim runs its own standalone CUPA). Several city fire depts are Participating Agencies running HMBP within their cities: Huntington Beach, Orange, Fountain Valley, Fullerton; LA County Fire runs La Habra's CalARP.
- CalARP is run by the County CUPA for all OC cities except Anaheim and La Habra.
