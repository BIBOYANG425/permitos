- **San Rafael Fire Department** — municipal department; operates seven neighborhood fire stations; Bureau of Fire Prevention enforces the San Rafael Fire Code and reviews Title 24 / local-ordinance requirements for building projects. — https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/departments/fire/
