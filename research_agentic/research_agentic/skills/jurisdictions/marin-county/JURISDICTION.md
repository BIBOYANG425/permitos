## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Marin County Public Works — Certified Unified Program Agency (CUPA)** — single countywide CUPA covering both incorporated cities and unincorporated areas (~850 regulated businesses). Administers all six Unified Program elements: HMBP, Hazardous Materials Management Plan/Inventory, Hazardous Waste Generator/Tiered Permitting, UST, Aboveground Petroleum Storage (APSA), and CalARP. Note: CUPA is housed in **Public Works**, not Environmental Health.
- https://publicworks.marincounty.gov/agencies/certified-unified-program-agency-cupa/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Marin County Fire Department (MCFD)** — county-operated, governed by the Board of Supervisors (County Service Area 31); serves unincorporated/West Marin (Woodacre, San Geronimo, Lagunitas, Nicasio, Point Reyes, Inverness, Tomales) from six stations. Not a CAL FIRE contract for structural service. — https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/fire
- Many incorporated areas are served by independent **fire protection districts** (Novato FPD, Southern Marin FPD, Kentfield FPD, Tiburon FPD, Bolinas FPD, Stinson Beach FPD, Sleepy Hollow FPD) and by municipal/JPA departments (San Rafael FD; Central Marin FD). See city files and: https://www.marinlafco.org/fire-protection-districts

## Building authority

- **Marin County Community Development Agency (CDA) — Building and Safety Division**, 3501 Civic Center Drive, Suite 308, San Rafael. Reviews plans, issues permits, conducts inspections for **unincorporated** Marin. — https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/cda/building-and-safety

## Notes

- GOTCHA (CUPA placement): Marin's CUPA sits in **Public Works**, not the typical county Environmental Health Division or a fire-department hazmat division. There is a **single countywide CUPA** with no separate city-run CUPAs verified — incorporated cities (incl. San Rafael, Novato) are served by the county Public Works CUPA.
- GOTCHA (fire consolidation): **Central Marin Fire Department** is a JPA serving the City of Larkspur and Town of Corte Madera (consolidated service); do not list those as standalone city departments. — https://www.centralmarinfire.org/
