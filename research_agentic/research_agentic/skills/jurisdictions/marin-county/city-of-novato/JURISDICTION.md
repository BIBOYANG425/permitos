> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Novato** (Marin County's second-largest city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs within city limits and where the city's adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **Marin County Public Works — Certified Unified Program Agency (CUPA)** (countywide). — https://publicworks.marincounty.gov/agencies/certified-unified-program-agency-cupa/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Novato Fire Protection District (NFPD)** — independent special district (not a city department) covering ~71 sq mi including the City of Novato, five stations. — https://www.novatofire.org/our-district/district-overview

## Building authority

- **City of Novato — Building Division** (Community Development); reviews plans, issues permits, conducts inspections. As of 2025-10-01 applications go through the "Novato Permits" online portal. — https://www.novato.gov/government/community-development/building-division

## Local code amendments

- Novato Municipal Code Chapter IV (Building and Housing) adopts the California Building Standards Code (Title 24); specific 2025-edition adopting ordinance URL UNVERIFIED. — https://library.municode.com/ca/novato

## Notes

- Fire AHJ is the **Novato Fire Protection District**, an independent district — Novato does not run a municipal fire department.
