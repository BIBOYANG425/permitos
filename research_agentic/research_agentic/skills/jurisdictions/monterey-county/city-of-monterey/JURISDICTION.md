## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by County of Monterey Health Department, Environmental Health Bureau (Hazardous Materials Management Services / HMMS).
- https://www.countyofmonterey.gov/government/departments-a-h/health/environmental-health/hazardous-materials-management/cupa-programs

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Monterey Fire Department (municipal department) — regional fire-service provider that also serves Pacific Grove, Carmel-by-the-Sea, and Sand City, plus federal/special-district installations (Presidio of Monterey, Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey Regional Airport District) by contract — https://monterey.gov/your_city_hall/departments/fire/administration.php

## Notes

- GOTCHA (regional fire provider): Monterey FD is the AHJ not just for the City of Monterey but for Pacific Grove, Carmel-by-the-Sea, and Sand City, plus federal installations under contract — so the responding/permitting fire authority for those jurisdictions is Monterey FD, not the county or a separate municipal department.
