> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of South San Francisco** (4th-largest city in San Mateo County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here and where the adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **San Mateo County Environmental Health Services (countywide CUPA)** for HMBP, hazardous waste generator, tiered permitting, UST, APSA, and CalARP.
- https://www.smchealth.org/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **South San Francisco Fire Department (SSFFD)** — own municipal department (founded 1908); operates a Fire Prevention Division handling construction/occupancy inspections and life-safety review from five stations. — https://www.ssfca.gov/Departments/Fire-Department

## Building authority

- **City of South San Francisco — Building Division / Economic & Community Development Department** — issues permits and performs inspections within the city. Specific division URL and adopted Title 24 edition UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: South San Francisco runs its **own municipal fire department** but is still regulated by the **county CUPA** for hazmat/hazwaste programs.
