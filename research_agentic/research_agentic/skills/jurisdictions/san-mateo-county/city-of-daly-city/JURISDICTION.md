> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Daly City** (largest city in San Mateo County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here and where the adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **San Mateo County Environmental Health Services (countywide CUPA)** for HMBP, hazardous waste generator, tiered permitting, UST, APSA, and CalARP.
- https://www.smchealth.org/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **North County Fire Authority (NCFA)** — Daly City does NOT run a standalone municipal fire department; fire/EMS is provided through the NCFA, a Joint Powers Authority of Brisbane, Daly City, and Pacifica (formed 2003). — https://northcountyfire.gov/ (city page: https://www.dalycity.org/461/Fire-Rescue)

## Building authority

- **City of Daly City — Building Division** — administers and enforces Title 24 for construction within the city. Daly City amendments live in Municipal Code Title 15 (Building and Construction). — https://www.dalycity.org/164/Building and https://library.municode.com/ca/daly_city

## Notes

- GOTCHA (fire): Daly City's fire AHJ is the **North County Fire Authority** JPA, not a "Daly City Fire Department." The same JPA serves Brisbane and Pacifica.
