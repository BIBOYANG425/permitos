> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of San Mateo** (2nd-largest city in San Mateo County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here and where the adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **San Mateo County Environmental Health Services (countywide CUPA)** for HMBP, hazardous waste generator, tiered permitting, UST, APSA, and CalARP.
- https://www.smchealth.org/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **San Mateo Consolidated Fire Department** — provides fire/EMS for the City of San Mateo (consolidated 2019, jointly serving Belmont, Foster City, and San Mateo). The City of San Mateo no longer runs a standalone department; service is via the consolidated agency. — https://www.smcfire.org/ (city page: https://www.cityofsanmateo.org/74/Fire)

## Building authority

- **City of San Mateo — Building Division / Community Development Department** — issues permits and performs inspections within the city. Specific division URL and adopted Title 24 edition UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA (fire): San Mateo's fire AHJ is the **San Mateo Consolidated Fire Department**, a consolidation also covering Belmont and Foster City; do not treat it as a single-city department.
