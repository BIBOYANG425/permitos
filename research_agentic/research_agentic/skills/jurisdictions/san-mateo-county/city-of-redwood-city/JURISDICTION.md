> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Redwood City** (San Mateo County seat; 3rd-largest city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here and where the adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **San Mateo County Environmental Health Services (countywide CUPA)** for HMBP, hazardous waste generator, tiered permitting, UST, APSA, and CalARP.
- https://www.smchealth.org/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Redwood City Fire Department (RCFD)** — own municipal department; ISO Class 1 rated (the only ISO Class 1 department in San Mateo County). Operates fire prevention / new-construction life-safety review for the city. — https://www.redwoodcity.org/departments/fire-department

## Building authority

- **City of Redwood City — Building Division / Community Development & Transportation** — issues permits and performs inspections within the city. Specific division URL and adopted Title 24 edition UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Redwood City runs its **own municipal fire department** (separate from the county and the regional JPAs/consolidations), but is still regulated by the **county CUPA** for hazmat/hazwaste programs.
