## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Colusa County Environmental Health (Community Development Department), the single county CUPA.
- https://www.countyofcolusaca.gov/426/Hazardous-Materials-Waste-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Williams Fire Protection Authority — a combination (career/volunteer) department created in 1994 via a joint powers agreement (JPA) between the City of Williams and the Williams Fire Protection District; serves the city and surrounding area.
- https://www.cityofwilliams.org/services/fire/index.php

## Building authority

- City of Williams Building and Housing Division — local building permits and code enforcement within city limits. (Confirm department contact/scope on the live city site.) — https://www.cityofwilliams.org

## Notes

- GOTCHA: fire protection in Williams is provided through a JPA (Williams Fire Protection Authority) jointly run by the City and the Williams Fire Protection District, not a standalone city fire department.
- The City of Williams does NOT run its own CUPA — hazmat/hazwaste/UST/APSA/CalARP are handled by the Colusa County Environmental Health CUPA.
