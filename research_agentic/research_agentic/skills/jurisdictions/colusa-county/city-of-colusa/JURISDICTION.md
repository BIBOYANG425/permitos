> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Colusa** (county seat of Colusa County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Colusa County Environmental Health (Community Development Department), the single county CUPA.
- https://www.countyofcolusaca.gov/426/Hazardous-Materials-Waste-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Colusa Fire Department — municipal combination department (paid staff plus volunteer roster); handles fire suppression, EMS, and fire prevention/commercial inspections for the city.
- https://cityofcolusa.com/fire-department/

## Building authority

- City of Colusa Building Department (Planning and Building) — issues permits, plan review, inspections, and certificates of occupancy within city limits.
- https://cityofcolusa.com/building/

## Notes

- GOTCHA: the City of Colusa runs its OWN municipal fire department and its OWN building department, but does NOT run its own CUPA — hazmat/hazwaste/UST/APSA/CalARP are handled by the Colusa County Environmental Health CUPA.
