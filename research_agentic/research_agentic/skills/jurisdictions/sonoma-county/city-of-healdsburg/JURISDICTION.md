## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Healdsburg/Sebastopol CUPA Program** — run by the **City of Healdsburg Fire Department** under the direction of the Fire Marshal. Via a **1994 Joint Powers Agreement**, this single CUPA regulates BOTH the City of Healdsburg AND the City of Sebastopol; Healdsburg FD is the point of contact. Administers HMBP, Hazardous Waste Generator, UST, Accidental Release Prevention (CalARP), Aboveground Petroleum Storage Tanks (APSA), and the hazardous-materials portions of the California Fire Code.
- https://healdsburg.gov/308/Certified-Unified-Program-Agencies-CUPA
