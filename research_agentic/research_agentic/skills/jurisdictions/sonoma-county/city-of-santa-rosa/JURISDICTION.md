> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Santa Rosa** (Sonoma County's largest city and county seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within city limits and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **City of Santa Rosa Fire Department** is its OWN CUPA (CalEPA-certified, CERS member agency) for facilities within city limits. Implements Health & Safety Code §25404 et seq. programs: HMBP/hazardous materials release response plans & inventory, hazardous materials management plan, hazardous waste control, UST, and risk management/prevention (CalARP). Contact: 707-543-3532.
- https://www.srcity.org/591/Hazardous-Materials---CUPA
- CUPA ordinance: Santa Rosa City Code Ch. 17-34 — https://ecode360.com/42966301

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Santa Rosa Fire Department (SRFD)** — municipal department; Fire Administration adjacent to Station 10 at 2373 Circadian Way. Handles fire prevention, hazmat response, and fire construction plan review/inspection. — https://www.srcity.org/546/Fire-Construction-Plan-Review-Inspection

## Building authority

- **City of Santa Rosa Building Division** reviews permit applications for State and local building code compliance and provides inspections; (707) 543-3200. — https://www.srcity.org/262/Building-Division
- Building code adopted in Santa Rosa City Code Title 18 (Buildings and Construction); confirm 2025 Title 24 edition adoption. — https://ecode360.com/42966430

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Santa Rosa runs its OWN CUPA through the Fire Department — facilities inside city limits do NOT report to Permit Sonoma's county CUPA. — https://www.srcity.org/591/Hazardous-Materials---CUPA
