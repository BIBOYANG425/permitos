> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Petaluma** (Sonoma County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within city limits and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **City of Petaluma Fire Department — Fire Prevention Bureau** is its OWN CUPA (CalEPA-certified) for facilities within city limits. Administers all six Unified Program elements: HMBP, Hazardous Waste Generator, UST, Aboveground Petroleum Storage Tanks (APSA), Accidental Release Prevention (CalARP), and the hazardous-materials portions of the California Fire Code. Located at 22 Bassett Street; Fire Prevention 707-778-4389.
- https://cityofpetaluma.org/fire-cupa/
- CUPA ordinance: Petaluma Municipal Code Ch. 17.21 — https://petaluma.municipal.codes/Code/17.21.040

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Petaluma Fire Department** — municipal department; Fire Prevention Bureau runs both fire prevention and the CUPA/hazmat program. — https://cityofpetaluma.org/fire-prevention-bureau/

## Building authority

- **City of Petaluma — Community Development Department, Building Division**, 11 English Street; (707) 778-4301. Oversees building construction/alteration for code compliance; permits via the Permits & Planning Hub. — https://cityofpetaluma.org/departments/building/

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Petaluma runs its OWN CUPA through the Fire Prevention Bureau — facilities inside city limits do NOT report to Permit Sonoma's county CUPA. — https://cityofpetaluma.org/fire-cupa/
