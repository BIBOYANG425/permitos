> Local EHS authority reference for **Riverside County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Riverside County Department of Environmental Health (DEH) — countywide CUPA
- https://rivcoeh.org/hazardous-materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Riverside County Fire Department — operated **under contract by CAL FIRE** (CAL FIRE/Riverside Unit, RRU); serves ~20 cities plus unincorporated areas — https://www.rvcfire.org

## Building authority

- Riverside County Building & Safety (Transportation & Land Management Agency). Title 24 adoption via county ordinance; municode URL UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: County Fire is a CAL FIRE contract operation, not a standalone county department.
- Participating Agencies under the County DEH CUPA: City of Riverside Fire (HMBP within city, since 1998) and City of Corona Fire. Neither city is its own CUPA.
