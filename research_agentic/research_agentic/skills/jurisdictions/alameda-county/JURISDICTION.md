> Local EHS authority reference for **Alameda County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Alameda County Department of Environmental Health (ACDEH) — CUPA for unincorporated areas and most cities, **including Oakland**
- https://health.alamedacountyca.gov/department/environmental-health-department

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Alameda County Fire Department (ACFD, formed 1993) — serves unincorporated areas + San Leandro, Dublin, Newark, Union City, Emeryville, and LBNL/LLNL — https://alamedacountyfire.gov

## Building authority

- Alameda County Community Development Agency — Building Inspection (unincorporated).

## Notes

- GOTCHA (CUPA decertification): the **City of Oakland CUPA was decertified by the state on 2015-01-05** — the first CUPA ever decertified in CA — and Oakland's facilities transferred to ACDEH in March 2015. Oakland is now under ACDEH, not its own CUPA.
- Cities that DO run their own CUPAs: Berkeley (Toxics Management Division), Fremont, Hayward, Livermore/Pleasanton, San Leandro, Union City.

## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (Bay Area AQMD)
- **Bay Area Air Quality Management District (BAAQMD)** controls air permits for all of Alameda County
- Permits: https://www.baaqmd.gov/permits | (415) 771-6000
- Key regulations: Regulation 2 (Permits), Rule 2-1 (Permit Requirements)

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA
- **Alameda County Environmental Health** is the CUPA for unincorporated areas
- Cities have own CUPAs: Oakland (Oakland Fire, (510) 238-3631), Berkeley (Berkeley Fire Prevention, (510) 981-5505)
- County CUPA: (510) 567-8251 | CERS: https://cers.calepa.ca.gov

### Wastewater
- **East Bay Municipal Utility District (EBMUD)** — major provider, industrial pretreatment program
- Industrial Waste Section: (510) 287-1000 | https://www.ebmud.com
- EBMUD has categorical limits and local limits for 30+ parameters

### Stormwater
- Alameda County Clean Water Program (unincorporated + cities under countywide MS4)
- http://www.cleanwaterprogram.org

### Fire Code
- Alameda County Fire Department (unincorporated + some cities)
- CFC adopted with local amendments
- Fire Prevention: (510) 667-3900

### Zoning
- Alameda County Planning Department (unincorporated)
- https://www.acgov.org/planning
