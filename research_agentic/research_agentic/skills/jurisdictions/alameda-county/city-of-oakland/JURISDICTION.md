## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **No city CUPA** — Oakland is served by Alameda County Department of Environmental Health (ACDEH)
- https://health.alamedacountyca.gov/department/environmental-health-department

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Oakland Fire Department (fire prevention / inspections).

## Building authority

- Oakland Planning & Building Dept. Oakland Municipal Code Title 15 (Title 24 adoption); municode URL UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: the Oakland CUPA (Oakland Fire Dept) was **decertified by the state on 2015-01-05** — the first CUPA ever decertified in CA — and its ~3,000 facilities transferred to ACDEH in March 2015. Do not treat Oakland as its own CUPA.
