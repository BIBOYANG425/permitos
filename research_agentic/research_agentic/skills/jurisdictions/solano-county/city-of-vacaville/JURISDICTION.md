> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Vacaville** (third-largest city in Solano County; ~100,000 residents). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs inside city limits and where the adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **Solano County Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division (HazMat Program)**, the countywide CUPA. HMBP / hazwaste / UST / APSA / CalARP for Vacaville facilities are handled by the County, not the City.
- https://www.solanocounty.gov/government/resource-management/environmental-health/hazardous-materials-hazmat-program

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **City of Vacaville Fire Department** — municipal department (~100 personnel, five stations) serving the City limits. NOTE: separate from the **Vacaville Fire Protection District (VFPD)**, an independent special district (~135 sq mi, four stations) that split off from the City in 1981 and serves the surrounding **unincorporated** area. The City FD is the AHJ inside city limits. — https://www.cityofvacaville.gov/government/fire-department  /  https://www.vfpd.net/

## Building authority

- **City of Vacaville Community Development Department — Building Division** reviews and inspects all construction within city limits and enforces Title 24. — https://www.cityofvacaville.gov/government/community-development/building

## Local code amendments

- City of Vacaville building code is codified at Municipal Code Ch. 15.20 (e.g., 15.20.252 "California Building Code Adopted and Amended"). The posted ordinance referenced the **2022 California Building Code** (2021 IBC); state law required adoption of the **2025 edition by 2026-01-01**, so confirm the currently operative edition on the live page. — https://www.codepublishing.com/CA/Vacaville/html/Vacaville15/Vacaville1520252.html

## Notes

- GOTCHA (city FD vs same-name district): **Vacaville Fire Department** (municipal, city limits) is NOT the **Vacaville Fire Protection District** (independent district covering surrounding unincorporated area). Do not conflate them when assigning the fire AHJ.
- Like all Solano cities, Vacaville is **not its own CUPA** — County Environmental Health is the CUPA.
