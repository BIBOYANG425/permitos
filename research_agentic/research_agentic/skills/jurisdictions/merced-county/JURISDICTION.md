## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Merced County Division of Environmental Health (MCDEH) — sole **countywide CUPA**; runs all six Unified Program elements (HMBP, CalARP, UST, APSA/Aboveground Petroleum Storage, Hazardous Waste Generator, Tiered Permitting/Hazardous Waste Treatment). No incorporated city in Merced County runs its own CUPA or is a separate Participating Agency. Submit via CERS.
- https://www.countyofmerced.com/1830/CUPA-Hazardous-Materials-Waste-Informati

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Merced County Fire Department — administered/operated by contract with CAL FIRE (since 1988); covers all unincorporated areas plus several incorporated cities. — https://www.countyofmerced.com/3883/Fire-Department
- Several cities run their own fire AHJ: City of Merced Fire Department (full municipal), City of Los Banos Fire Department (full municipal), City of Atwater (city dept staffed by CAL FIRE under a cooperative agreement).

## Notes

- GOTCHA: The county CUPA (MCDEH, Environmental Health) is separate from the county Fire Department. Despite three cities having municipal fire departments, the CUPA for hazmat/HMBP/UST/APSA/CalARP/hazwaste is the **county Environmental Health division countywide** — no city fire dept runs the CUPA.
- GOTCHA: Merced County Fire is a CAL FIRE contract county (since 1988), not a standalone county fire agency. The City of Atwater fire dept is also CAL FIRE-staffed under a separate city cooperative agreement (since ~2008), so two distinct CAL FIRE contracts operate in the county.
