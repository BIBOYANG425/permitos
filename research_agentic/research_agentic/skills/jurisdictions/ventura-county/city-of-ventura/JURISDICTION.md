## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — City of Ventura Fire Department is a **Participating Agency** implementing HMBP, APSA/SPCC, CalARP, and the UST program within the city, under the Ventura County RMA CUPA
- https://www.cityofventura.ca.gov/182/CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Ventura City Fire Department.

## Building authority

- City of San Buenaventura Community Development — Building & Safety. Code amendments UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: a **Participating Agency**, not a full CUPA — the certified CUPA of record is Ventura County RMA. Contrast with the City of Oxnard, which IS its own full CUPA.
- Officially named San Buenaventura; addresses usually read "Ventura, CA".
