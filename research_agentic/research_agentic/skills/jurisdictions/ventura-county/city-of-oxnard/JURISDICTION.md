## CUPA (Unified Program)

- City of Oxnard Fire Department — its **own full CUPA**, implementing all six unified programs (HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA/AST, CalARP, Cal Fire Code hazmat) within the city
- https://www.oxnard.gov/fire-department/certified-unified-program-agency-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Oxnard Fire Department.

## Building authority

- City of Oxnard Development Services — Building & Engineering. Code amendments UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Oxnard is a **full CUPA**, not a Participating Agency — do not route it to the Ventura County RMA CUPA. (Its county neighbor San Buenaventura/Ventura, by contrast, is only a Participating Agency.)

## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (VCAPCD)
- **Controlling air district**: Ventura County APCD (VCAPCD) — all permit/exemption rules apply
- **Oxnard CUPA does NOT handle air permits** — air is handled exclusively by VCAPCD
- VCAPCD permit applications: https://www.vcapcd.org/permitting
- Oxnard industrial areas: CA-1 (Oxnard Plain) — in VCAPCD jurisdiction

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA (HMBP, CalARP, UST, APSA)
- **Oxnard Fire Department is the CUPA** — full authority for all 6 unified programs
- HMBP filing via CERS (https://cers.calepa.ca.gov) — Oxnard Fire reviews submissions
- CUPA contact: Oxnard Fire Prevention Bureau, (805) 385-7750
- Inspection program: annual for larger facilities, biennial for smaller
- CalARP: Oxnard FD is the local implementing agency; coordinate with them for RMP submissions
- UST: Oxnard FD Underground Storage Tank Program

### Wastewater / Industrial Pretreatment
- **City of Oxnard IWD (Industrial Waste Discharge) Permit**
  - Controlling authority: City of Oxnard Environmental Resources Division
  - Phone: (805) 385-7880
  - Required for any industrial discharge to the Oxnard sewer system above domestic strength
  - Apply via: City of Oxnard Environmental Resources, 305 W. Third Street, Oxnard CA 93030
  - Typical parameters regulated: BOD, TSS, O&G, metals, pH, flow
  - Annual permit, fees based on discharge volume and strength [VERIFY current fees]
- Oxnard WPCF (Water Pollution Control Facility): final treatment before ocean discharge under NPDES permit

### Stormwater
- Oxnard has separate stormwater permit requirements — within the Ventura County NPDES Municipal Separate Storm Sewer System (MS4) Permit
- Industrial facilities: must comply with State IGP; contact Oxnard Public Works for local MS4 coordination
- Oxnard Stormwater Hotline: (805) 385-7537

### Fire Code / High-Pile / Hazmat Operational Permit
- **Oxnard Fire Department** is the AHJ for fire code compliance
- Oxnard has adopted the **California Fire Code** (CFC) with local amendments [VERIFY adoption ordinance]
- Hazmat operational permits: apply to Oxnard Fire Prevention Bureau
- High-piled storage permits: Oxnard Fire Prevention Bureau, (805) 385-7750
- Fire permit fee schedule: https://www.oxnard.gov/fire-department [VERIFY current schedule]

### Zoning / Land Use
- City of Oxnard Development Services, Planning Division
- Zoning code: Oxnard Municipal Code Title 16
- Industrial zones in Oxnard: M-1 (Light Industrial), M-2 (General Industrial), H-C (Heavy Commercial)
- CUP applications: Planning Division, (805) 385-7858
- Online portal: https://www.oxnard.gov/planning
