> Local EHS authority reference for **Ventura County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Ventura County Resource Management Agency (RMA), Environmental Health Division — countywide CUPA
- https://rma.venturacounty.gov/divisions/environmental-health/cupa-certified-unified-program-agency/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Ventura County Fire Department — unincorporated areas + most cities — https://vcfd.org

## Building authority

- Ventura County RMA Building & Safety. Local amendments via Ventura County Ordinance Code Div. 8 (Title 24).

## Notes

- GOTCHA (two cities carved out, but NOT equally): the County RMA CUPA runs the unified program countywide **except the City of Oxnard and the City of San Buenaventura (Ventura)** — but they differ:
  - **City of Oxnard** is its **own full CUPA** (Oxnard Fire Dept implements all six unified programs).
  - **City of San Buenaventura (Ventura)** is a **Participating Agency** (HMBP/APSA/CalARP/UST) under the County RMA CUPA — NOT its own CUPA.
- Hazardous substances ordinance: Ventura County Ordinance Code Division 4, Public Health, Chapter 5.

## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (VCAPCD)
- **Ventura County Air Pollution Control District (VCAPCD)** is the controlling air authority for ALL of Ventura County including unincorporated areas and all cities
- Permits: https://www.vcapcd.org/permitting
- Key rules: Rule 10 (permit to operate), Rule 23 (exemptions), Rule 74.19 (graphic arts)
- VCAPCD contact: (805) 645-1400

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA
- **Ventura County RMA Environmental Health** is the CUPA for unincorporated areas and most cities
- EXCEPTION: City of Oxnard (own CUPA) and City of Ventura (Participating Agency under County)
- CUPA contact: RMA Environmental Health, (805) 654-2813
- HMBP via CERS: https://cers.calepa.ca.gov
- UST Program: RMA Environmental Health, same contact

### Wastewater / Industrial Pretreatment  
- **Ventura County Watershed Protection District** for unincorporated area stormwater
- Individual city utilities for sewer/wastewater — each city has its own pretreatment program
- For unincorporated industrial areas, contact the serving utility district

### Stormwater (Unincorporated)
- Covered under Ventura County MS4 Permit (State Board Order)
- County Public Works Agency — Watershed Protection Division
- Contact: (805) 654-2021

### Fire Code
- **Ventura County Fire Protection District (VCFD)** covers unincorporated areas and most cities
- EXCEPTION: City of Oxnard (Oxnard Fire Dept), City of Ventura (Ventura City Fire Dept)
- VCFD fire permits and CFC compliance: https://vcfd.org
- VCFD has adopted CFC with local amendments per Ventura County Ordinance

### Zoning (Unincorporated)
- Ventura County Resource Management Agency, Planning Division
- Ventura County Non-Coastal Zoning Ordinance (Ord. No. 4303)
- Industrial uses: Industrial-compatible zoning (I designation)
- Contact: (805) 654-2488
