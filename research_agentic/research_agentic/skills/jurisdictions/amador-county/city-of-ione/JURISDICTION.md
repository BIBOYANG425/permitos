> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Ione** (largest incorporated city in Amador County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Amador County Environmental Health Department (county CUPA for all of Amador County).
- https://www.amadorcounty.gov/departments/environmental-health/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Ione Fire Department — municipal combination department (became the City of Ione Fire Department in 1952), all-risk response; operates a hazardous materials unit. 22 W Jackson St., Ione.
- https://www.ione.ca.gov/fire-department

## Building authority

- City of Ione Building Department / Building Inspection — handles building permits and inspections within the city.
- https://www.ione.ca.gov/building

## Notes

- Ione is the largest incorporated city in Amador County and runs its OWN municipal fire department.
