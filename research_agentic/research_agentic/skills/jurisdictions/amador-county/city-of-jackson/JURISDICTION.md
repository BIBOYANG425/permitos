> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Jackson** (Amador County seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Amador County Environmental Health Department (county CUPA for all of Amador County).
- https://www.amadorcounty.gov/departments/environmental-health/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Jackson Fire Department — municipal combination department (career + volunteer), serves the city and portions of unincorporated Amador County. 33 Broadway, Jackson.
- https://www.ci.jackson.ca.us/fire_department.php

## Building authority

- City of Jackson Building Department — issues building permits and performs inspections within the city. 33 Broadway, Jackson.
- https://www.ci.jackson.ca.us/building_department.php

## Notes

- Jackson is the Amador County seat and one of the county's larger incorporated cities; it runs its OWN municipal fire department (not CAL FIRE/county).
