## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Kings County Department of Public Health, Environmental Health Services (EHS) — the county's Certified Unified Program Agency, running the CUPA/Hazardous Waste program (HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, CalARP) via CERS. No incorporated city in Kings County operates its own CUPA — all four cities (Hanford, Lemoore, Corcoran, Avenal) are served by the county CUPA.
- https://www.kcdph.com/cupahazwaste

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Kings County Fire Department — serves the unincorporated areas and, by contract, the Cities of Avenal and Corcoran; affiliated with the CAL FIRE Fresno-Kings Unit. https://www.countyofkingsca.gov/departments/public-safety/fire-department
- City of Hanford runs its own municipal Hanford Fire Department (career staffing). https://www.cityofhanfordca.com/203/Fire
- City of Lemoore has its own city fire department. UNVERIFIED (current staffing/structure)
