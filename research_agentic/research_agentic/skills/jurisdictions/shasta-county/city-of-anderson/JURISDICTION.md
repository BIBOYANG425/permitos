> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Anderson** (Shasta County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs within the city. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Shasta County Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA) — https://www.shastacounty.gov/environmental-health/page/hazardous-materials-program

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Anderson Fire Protection District — independent special district (not a city department, not Shasta County Fire) serving the City of Anderson, 1925 Howard Street, Anderson; phone (530) 378-6699 — https://www.ci.anderson.ca.us/departments/anderson_fire_department/index.php

## Building authority

- City of Anderson Development Services & Building Department, Building Division — issues and inspects building, plumbing, mechanical, and electrical permits — https://www.ci.anderson.ca.us/departments/development_services_and_building_departments/building_department/index.php

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Fire service for Anderson is provided by the **Anderson Fire Protection District**, a special district that predates the city's 1956 incorporation — it is not a municipal city fire department and not Shasta County Fire.
- Anderson runs its own building division, but Unified Program/hazmat compliance is handled by the **Shasta County** CUPA.
