## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Shasta County Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division (EHD) — the **single CUPA for the entire county including all incorporated cities** (Redding, Anderson, Shasta Lake). Administers HMBP/HMRRP, hazardous waste generator & treatment, UST, APSA aboveground petroleum storage, and CalARP/accidental release prevention. No city in Shasta County runs its own CUPA. Office at 1855 Placer Street, Suite 201, Redding, CA 96001; phone (530) 225-5787.
- https://www.shastacounty.gov/environmental-health/page/hazardous-materials-program
- CalEPA CERS regulator entry ("Shasta County Environmental Health", type CUPA): https://cersapps.calepa.ca.gov/Public/Directory/RegulatorDetails/1067

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- CAL FIRE Shasta-Trinity Unit / Shasta County Fire Department — combination department; CAL FIRE and Shasta County Fire operate as an integrated unit serving the unincorporated county (State Responsibility Area + local). HQ at 875 Cypress Avenue, Redding — https://www.shastacounty.gov/fire/page/shasta-county-fire-department
- City of Redding Fire Department — independent municipal department (eight stations) serving the City of Redding — https://www.cityofredding.gov/government/departments/fire_department/index.php
- Anderson Fire Protection District — special district (not Shasta County Fire) serving the City of Anderson and surrounding area, 1925 Howard Street, Anderson — https://www.ci.anderson.ca.us/departments/anderson_fire_department/index.php
- Shasta Lake Fire Protection District — special district serving the City of Shasta Lake, 4126 Ashby Court, Shasta Lake — https://www.shastalakefpd.gov/

## Building authority

- Shasta County Department of Resource Management, Building Division (unincorporated county only) — phone (530) 225-5761 — https://www.shastacounty.gov/building

## Local code amendments

- Shasta County Code Title 16 (Buildings and Construction) adopts the California Building Standards Code, Title 24. The County has posted notice of the 2025 edition effective 2026-01-01 — confirm the currently adopted edition on the live page. Code: https://library.municode.com/ca/shasta_county/codes/code — 2025 code notice: https://www.shastacounty.gov/building/page/2025-california-building-standards-code

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **No city in Shasta County runs its own CUPA.** Shasta County Environmental Health is the sole CUPA countywide, including Redding, Anderson, and Shasta Lake.
- GOTCHA: Fire AHJ is fragmented. The county's three incorporated cities are NOT served by Shasta County Fire — Redding has its own municipal fire department, while Anderson and Shasta Lake are each served by an independent **Fire Protection District** (special district), not a city department and not the county. Shasta County Fire / CAL FIRE Shasta-Trinity Unit covers the unincorporated county.
