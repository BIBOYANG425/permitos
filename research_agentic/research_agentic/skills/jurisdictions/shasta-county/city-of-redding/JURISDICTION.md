> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Redding** (Shasta County's largest city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs within the city. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Shasta County Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA) — https://www.shastacounty.gov/environmental-health/page/hazardous-materials-program

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Redding Fire Department — independent municipal department, eight stations, HQ at 777 Cypress Avenue, Redding — https://www.cityofredding.gov/government/departments/fire_department/index.php

## Building authority

- City of Redding Development Services, Building Division — Permit Center, 777 Cypress Avenue, 1st Floor; phone (530) 225-4030 — https://www.cityofredding.gov/government/departments/development_services/building/index.php

## Notes

- Redding runs its own municipal fire department and its own building division, but hazmat/Unified Program compliance is still handled by the **Shasta County** CUPA.
