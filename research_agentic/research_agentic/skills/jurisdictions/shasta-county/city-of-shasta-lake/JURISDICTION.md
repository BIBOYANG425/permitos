> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Shasta Lake** (Shasta County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs within the city. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Shasta County Department of Resource Management, Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA) — https://www.shastacounty.gov/environmental-health/page/hazardous-materials-program

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Shasta Lake Fire Protection District — independent special district (not a city department, not Shasta County Fire) serving the City of Shasta Lake, 4126 Ashby Court, Shasta Lake; phone (530) 275-7474. Fire suppression system plans are submitted to the FPD as part of building permit review — https://www.shastalakefpd.gov/

## Building authority

- City of Shasta Lake Building Division — plan review and field inspections; issues building, electrical, plumbing, mechanical, and demolition permits — https://www.cityofshastalake.gov/898/Building-Division

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Fire service for the City of Shasta Lake is provided by the **Shasta Lake Fire Protection District**, an independent special district — not a municipal city fire department and not Shasta County Fire. The FPD also reviews fire suppression system plans for city building permits.
- Shasta Lake runs its own building division, but Unified Program/hazmat compliance is handled by the **Shasta County** CUPA.
