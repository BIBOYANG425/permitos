> Local EHS authority reference for **Santa Clara County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- County of Santa Clara Dept of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Compliance Division (HMCD) — CUPA for San José, Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, Milpitas, Monte Sereno, Morgan Hill, Saratoga, and unincorporated areas
- https://hazmat.sccgov.org/home

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Santa Clara County Fire Department — serves Campbell, Cupertino, Los Altos, Los Altos Hills, Los Gatos, Monte Sereno, Saratoga, San Martin + unincorporated — https://www.sccfd.org

## Building authority

- Santa Clara County Dept of Planning & Development (unincorporated).

## Notes

- GOTCHA (multiple CUPAs): the county has FOUR CUPAs — County DEH HMCD, City of Gilroy, City of Santa Clara, City of Sunnyvale.
- GOTCHA: **San José (3rd-largest CA city) does NOT run its own CUPA** — it falls under County DEH HMCD.

## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (Bay Area AQMD)
- **Bay Area AQMD** covers all of Santa Clara County
- https://www.baaqmd.gov | (415) 771-6000

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA
- **Santa Clara County Public Health** is the CUPA for unincorporated areas
- City of San Jose: CUPA is San Jose Fire Dept, (408) 535-7770
- County CUPA: (408) 918-3400 | CERS: https://cers.calepa.ca.gov

### Wastewater
- **San Jose / Santa Clara Regional Wastewater Facility** — major POTW
- Industrial Waste Permit: (408) 945-3000 | https://www.sanjoseca.gov/iws
- Significant semiconductor industry — specific limits for copper, fluoride, solvents

### Stormwater
- Santa Clara Valley Urban Runoff Pollution Prevention Program (SCVURPPP)
- Countywide MS4 permit
- https://www.scvurppp.org

### Fire Code
- Santa Clara County Fire Dept (unincorporated + some cities)
- City of San Jose Fire Dept: (408) 535-7770

### Zoning
- County: Planning Office, (408) 299-5770
- City of San Jose: Planning, Building & Code Enforcement, (408) 535-3555
