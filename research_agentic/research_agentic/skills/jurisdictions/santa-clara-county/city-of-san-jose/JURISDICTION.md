> Local EHS authority reference for **City of San José**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; confirm the adopted Title 24 edition (2025 code effective 2026-01-01).

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **No city CUPA** — San José is served by the County of Santa Clara Dept of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Compliance Division (HMCD)
- https://hazmat.sccgov.org/home

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- San José Fire Department, Bureau of Fire Prevention (fire/hazmat code enforcement; not the CUPA) — https://www.sanjoseca.gov

## Building authority

- San José Planning, Building & Code Enforcement — adopts the **San José Building Code** (CBC-based) via Municipal Code Title 24
- https://www.sanjoseca.gov/your-government/departments-offices/planning-building-code-enforcement/building-division/adopted-building-codes
- Municode: https://library.municode.com/ca/san_jose/codes/code_of_ordinances

## Notes

- GOTCHA: the 3rd-largest CA city does **NOT** run its own CUPA — hazmat regulation is County HMCD. The SJFD prevention bureau handles fire-code enforcement only.
