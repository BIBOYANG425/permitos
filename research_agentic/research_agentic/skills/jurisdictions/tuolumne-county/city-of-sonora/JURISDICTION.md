## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **Tuolumne County Division of Environmental Health** (county Community Development Department), the countywide CUPA for HMBP/UST/hazwaste-generator/APSA.
- https://www.tuolumnecounty.ca.gov/1555/Hazardous-Materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **City of Sonora Fire Department** — the city runs its OWN municipal fire department (it is a member jurisdiction of the Tuolumne County Fire Authority JPA, but provides fire service within city limits). https://sonoraca.com/

## Building authority

- **City of Sonora Community Development Department** (Building + Planning; 94 N. Washington Street, Sonora) — enforces the California Building Code via plan check and inspection — https://sonoraca.com/city-services/departments/community-development/

## Local code amendments

- City building/construction provisions are in **Title 15 (Buildings and Construction)** of the Sonora Municipal Code on Municode — https://library.municode.com/ca/sonora — specific adopted CBC edition (2022 vs 2025) UNVERIFIED from live pages reached.

## Notes

- Sonora is the only incorporated city in Tuolumne County, so it is the only jurisdiction inside the county with its own municipal fire department and its own building department; the surrounding unincorporated county uses TCFD/CAL FIRE and the county Building and Safety Division.
