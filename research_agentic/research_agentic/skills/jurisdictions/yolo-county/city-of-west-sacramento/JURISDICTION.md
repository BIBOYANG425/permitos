> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of West Sacramento** (Yolo County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs inside city limits. West Sacramento is distinctive because it runs the Unified Program itself as a Participating Agency. Verify the live page before quoting.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **City of West Sacramento Fire Department — Community Risk Reduction (CUPA / Hazardous Materials Program)** runs the Unified Program inside West Sacramento city limits as a **Participating Agency (PA)** under the Yolo County CUPA umbrella. It handles HMBP/CERS reporting, inspections, and permits for facilities within the City. Programs administered via the PA arrangement include HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, and CalARP — confirm the exact element split with the County CUPA on the live page. — https://www.cityofwestsacramento.org/government/departments/fire/community-risk-reduction/hazardous-materials-program/cupa-program
- County CUPA umbrella (oversight): **Yolo County Environmental Health Division** — https://www.yolocounty.gov/government/general-government-departments/community-services/environmental-health-division/hazardous-materials-programs

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **City of West Sacramento Fire Department** — municipal fire department; same department that houses the CUPA/hazmat program. — https://www.cityofwestsacramento.org/government/departments/fire

## Building authority

- **City of West Sacramento** building/permit services issue permits and inspect construction within city limits (NOT the County). — https://www.cityofwestsacramento.org/government/departments

## Notes

- GOTCHA (own Participating Agency): unlike Davis, Woodland, and Winters (which are served directly by Yolo County EH), **West Sacramento Fire runs the Unified Program itself as a PA**. A West Sacramento facility's HMBP/inspection point of contact is the City FD, not County EH. — https://www.cityofwestsacramento.org/government/departments/fire/community-risk-reduction/hazardous-materials-program/cupa-program
