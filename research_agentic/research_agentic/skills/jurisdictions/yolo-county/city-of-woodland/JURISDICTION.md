> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Woodland** (Yolo County seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs inside city limits. Verify the live page before quoting.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **Yolo County Environmental Health Division (Hazardous Materials Programs)**, which is also headquartered in Woodland (292 West Beamer Street, Woodland, CA 95695; (530) 666-8646). Woodland facilities report HMBP/CERS, hazardous waste, UST, APSA, and CalARP through Yolo County EH. — https://www.yolocounty.gov/government/general-government-departments/community-services/environmental-health-division/hazardous-materials-programs

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **City of Woodland Fire Department** — municipal fire department serving the City of Woodland. — https://www.cityofwoodland.gov/

## Building authority

- **City of Woodland** building/permit division issues permits and inspects construction within city limits (NOT the County). — https://www.cityofwoodland.gov/

## Notes

- Woodland is the Yolo County seat and the location of the County's Environmental Health / CUPA and Building offices, but the City still runs its own municipal fire department and its own building permitting; the County CUPA serves city facilities. None of this makes Woodland its own CUPA.
