> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Davis** (Yolo County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs inside city limits. Verify the live page before quoting.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by **Yolo County Environmental Health Division (Hazardous Materials Programs)**. Davis facilities report HMBP/CERS, hazardous waste, UST, APSA, and CalARP through Yolo County EH (292 West Beamer Street, Woodland, CA 95695; (530) 666-8646). — https://www.yolocounty.gov/government/general-government-departments/community-services/environmental-health-division/hazardous-materials-programs

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **City of Davis Fire Department** — municipal fire department serving the City of Davis. — https://www.cityofdavis.org/city-hall/welcome-to-the-fire-department
- Note: the adjacent **UC Davis campus** is served by its own **UC Davis Fire Department**, a separate entity (and UC Davis leads the county multi-agency hazmat team). Hazmat regulatory CUPA authority over the campus still rests with Yolo County EH. — https://fire.ucdavis.edu/

## Building authority

- **City of Davis** building/permit division issues permits and inspects construction within city limits (NOT the County). — https://www.cityofdavis.org/

## Notes

- GOTCHA (city FD vs UC Davis FD): the **Davis Fire Department** (City) and the **UC Davis Fire Department** (campus) are distinct agencies with their own jurisdictions, though they share automatic/mutual aid. Do not conflate them. — https://fire.ucdavis.edu/
