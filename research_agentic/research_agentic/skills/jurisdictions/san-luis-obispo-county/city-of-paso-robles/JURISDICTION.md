> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of El Paso de Robles (Paso Robles)**, the second-largest city in San Luis Obispo County. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs inside the city and where its adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the County of San Luis Obispo Environmental Health Services CUPA (HMBP, hazwaste generator, UST, APSA/AST, CalARP). The Paso Robles Fire Department provides hazmat emergency response (via the regional hazmat team) but is not the permitting CUPA. County CUPA: https://www.slocounty.ca.gov/departments/health-agency/public-health/environmental-health-services/all-environmental-health-services/cupa-program-hazardous-materials-waste
