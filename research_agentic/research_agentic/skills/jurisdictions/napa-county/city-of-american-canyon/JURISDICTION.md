> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of American Canyon** (Napa County's second-largest city, ~21,000 residents, southern Napa County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs inside city limits and where adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **Napa County Division of Environmental Health (DEH)**, the single countywide CUPA. — https://www.napacounty.gov/1919/CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **American Canyon Fire Protection District (ACFPD)** — independent fire protection district, maintained as a **subsidiary special district of the City of American Canyon** since the City incorporated in 1992 (district originally formed under the Napa County Board of Supervisors). Two stations; covers the City plus adjoining unincorporated southern Napa County. Provides/receives mutual aid with Napa County Fire/CAL FIRE Sonoma-Lake-Napa Unit and Solano County agencies. — https://www.amcanfire.ca.gov/Residents/About-the-District

## Building authority

- **City of American Canyon — Community Development Department / Building (and Safety) Division** issues building permits within city limits. — https://www.americancanyon.gov/Work/Community-Infrastructure-Development/Community-Development/Building-Division

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Fire service is the **American Canyon Fire Protection District**, a subsidiary district of the City — not the county CAL FIRE department and not a typical city-line fire department. Treat ACFPD as the AHJ.
