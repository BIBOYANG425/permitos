> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Calexico** (border city, second-largest in Imperial County, ~40,000). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here vs. at the county/state level. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **DTSC Imperial County CUPA** (the state runs the CUPA for all of Imperial County). https://dtsc.ca.gov/imperial-county-cupa/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Calexico Fire Department** — the city's own municipal fire department (Fire Chief reports to the City Manager). Provides hazmat response and participates in the countywide Hazardous Emergency Response Team (HEAT) under a JPA with Imperial County. https://www.calexico.ca.gov/index.asp?SEC=AD50BA87-05CF-4B57-9A0E-9688E3616E13&Type=B_BASIC

## Building authority

- City of Calexico building/development services. Department page UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- Calexico runs its own fire department and is a HEAT JPA partner, but relies on the DTSC Imperial CUPA for HMBP / hazwaste / UST / APSA / CalARP.
