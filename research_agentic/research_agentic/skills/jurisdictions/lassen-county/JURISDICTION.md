## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Lassen County Environmental Health Department — single CUPA for the entire county; administers all six Unified Program elements (HMBP, hazardous waste generator/tiered treatment, UST, APSA, CalARP, CERS reporting) via a consolidated single inspection. No participating-agency split listed; the lone incorporated city (Susanville) is served by the county CUPA. Office at 1445 Paul Bunyan Road, Susanville, CA 96130.
- https://www.lassencounty.org/dept/environmental-health/hazardous-material-management
- CalEPA Regulator Directory confirmation: https://cersapps.calepa.ca.gov/Public/Directory/RegulatorDetails/1028

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- No single countywide structural fire department. Unincorporated communities are served by numerous independent fire protection districts / volunteer departments (e.g., Susan River FPD, Janesville FPD, Doyle FPD, Standish-Litchfield FPD, Northwest Lassen County FPD, Madeline FPD, Milford FD, Big Valley FPD, Westwood FD). The City of Susanville runs its own municipal fire department (see city file). State Responsibility Area wildland protection falls under CAL FIRE Lassen-Modoc Unit (covers Lassen, Modoc, Plumas and portions of Shasta/Siskiyou).
- https://www.fire.ca.gov/ (CAL FIRE) — Lassen-Modoc Unit
- https://www.lassencounty.org/

## Building authority

- Lassen County Department of Planning and Building Services, Building Division — building permits, plan checks, inspections for unincorporated areas. Office at 707 Nevada Street, Suite 5, Susanville, CA 96130.
- https://www.lassencounty.org/dept/planning-and-building-services/planning-and-building-services

## Local code amendments

- Lassen County Code Title 12 (Buildings and Construction) adopts the California Building Standards Code (Title 24) — published on eCode360. The code references the "most recent versions" of Title 24 Parts 2, 2.5, 3, 5, etc., with local amendments; eCode360 shows legislation current through Ordinance No. 2025-08 (adopted 2025-11-18). ## Notes

- GOTCHA (single incorporated city): Susanville is the **only** incorporated city in Lassen County (county seat). Every other community (Janesville, Doyle, Westwood, Bieber, Standish, etc.) is unincorporated and falls under county authority for CUPA and building, with fire service fragmented across many local fire protection districts.
- Large federal-land footprint (Lassen National Forest, BLM, plus the Lassen Volcanic National Park boundary nearby) — expect federal-land carve-outs where USFS/BLM/NPS, not the county, is the land manager.
