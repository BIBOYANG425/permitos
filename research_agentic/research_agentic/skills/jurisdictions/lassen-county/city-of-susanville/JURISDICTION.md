> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Susanville** (Lassen County seat and the county's only incorporated city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Lassen County Environmental Health Department (the countywide CUPA for HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, CalARP, CERS).
- https://www.lassencounty.org/dept/environmental-health/hazardous-material-management

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Susanville Fire Department — a municipal fire department operated by the City of Susanville with career staffing (Public Safety Chief, Assistant Chief, captains, engineers, firefighters, plus seasonal hires); a separate Susanville Volunteer Fire Department supplements it. Serves the city of Susanville. Headquartered at 1505 Main Street, Susanville, CA 96130.
- https://www.cityofsusanville.gov/191/Fire-Department

## Building authority

- City of Susanville — building permits handled through the City (Public Works / city website). Confirm the specific division on the live site.
- https://www.cityofsusanville.gov/

## Notes

- Susanville is the only incorporated city in Lassen County. It runs its OWN municipal fire department but is NOT its own CUPA — hazardous-materials / Unified Program oversight comes from the county Environmental Health CUPA.
