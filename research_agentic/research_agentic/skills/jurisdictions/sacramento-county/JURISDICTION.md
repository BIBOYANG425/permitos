> Local EHS authority reference for **Sacramento County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Sacramento County Environmental Management Department (EMD), Environmental Compliance Division — CUPA for the **entire county including the City of Sacramento**
- https://emd.saccounty.gov/EC/CUPA/Pages/default.aspx

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Sacramento Metropolitan Fire District ("Metro Fire", formed 2000 from 16 predecessor depts) — unincorporated areas + Citrus Heights and Rancho Cordova — https://metrofire.ca.gov
- City of Sacramento Fire Department — the city itself — https://www.cityofsacramento.gov/fire

## Building authority

- Sacramento County Community Development Dept — Building Permits & Inspection — https://development.saccounty.gov

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **the City of Sacramento does NOT run its own CUPA** — the whole county is under County EMD.
- Do not confuse with the City of West Sacramento (Yolo County), which runs its own CUPA via its Fire Dept.
