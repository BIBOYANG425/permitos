> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Bishop** (Inyo County's only incorporated city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Inyo County Department of Environmental Health Services (ICDEHS), the countywide CUPA / Administering Agency. The county reports it coordinates with City of Bishop staff on new businesses, but the regulatory CUPA authority is the county's.
- https://www.inyocounty.us/services/environmental-health/environmental-health-programs/hazardous-materials-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Bishop Fire Department — a joint operation (cooperation) between the City of Bishop and the Bishop (Rural) Fire Protection District, governed by both the City Council and the District Board. Primarily volunteer with some career staff; serves Bishop, West Bishop, North Bishop, Laws, Wilkerson, Rocking K, and the Bishop Paiute Reservation (under contract with the Tribe). Wildland SRA protection is CAL FIRE San Bernardino Unit (BDU).
- https://cityofbishop.ca.gov/departments/fire/index.php

## Building authority

- City of Bishop Building Department (within Planning and Public Works), 377 West Line Street, Bishop, CA 93514; (760) 873-8458 / (760) 873-5863. The city processes its own building permits and (per the city) moved to an online permit portal starting July 2025.
- https://cityofbishop.ca.gov/departments/public_works/building_permits.php

## Notes

- The City of Bishop building authority is distinct from the county's — within city limits, the City (not Inyo County Building & Safety) issues building permits. Confirm the city's currently adopted Title 24 edition (UNVERIFIED) given the 2025 statewide edition change on 2026-01-01.
- The Bishop Fire Department is not a standalone municipal fire department; it is jointly governed with the Bishop FPD.
