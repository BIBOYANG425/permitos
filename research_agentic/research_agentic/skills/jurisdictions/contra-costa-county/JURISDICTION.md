## Notes

- GOTCHA (unusual local rule): CCHHMP administers a county **Industrial Safety Ordinance (ISO)** — stricter than state CalARP for refineries/chemical plants — for the County **and a separate ISO for the City of Richmond**. Research the ISO in addition to CalARP for major chemical/refinery facilities.
- GOTCHA (consolidation): Con Fire **annexed the East Contra Costa Fire Protection District on 2022-07-01**, extending into Brentwood/Oakley/far East County. Do not list ECCFPD as separate.
