Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Fresno County Department of Public Health, Environmental Health Division — CUPA for the **entire county including the City of Fresno** (certified 1996)
- https://www.fresnocountyca.gov/Departments/Public-Health/Environmental-Health/HazMat-Compliance-The-Designated-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Fresno Fire Department (city) — https://www.fresno.gov/fire/
- Fresno County Fire Protection District (unincorporated, CAL FIRE-affiliated)

## Building authority

- Fresno County Public Works & Planning — Building (unincorporated). Code amendments UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **the City of Fresno does NOT run its own CUPA** — Fresno City Fire's hazmat team is emergency-response only (HMRT), not the regulatory CUPA. The County DPH EH is the designated CUPA countywide.
