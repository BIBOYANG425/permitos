## CUPA (Unified Program)

- San Benito County Health Department, Environmental Health Division — single countywide CUPA (no city runs its own CUPA; Hollister is served by the county CUPA)
- https://cersapps.calepa.ca.gov/Public/Directory/RegulatorDetails/1052

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Structural fire/EMS in the unincorporated county is provided **by contract by the Hollister Fire Department** (which also covers San Juan Bautista). State-responsibility-area wildland falls under CAL FIRE San Benito-Monterey Unit (BEU). — https://hollister.ca.gov/

## Building authority

- San Benito County Planning & Building / Resource Management Agency. Exact division name and code-adoption edition UNVERIFIED.

## Notes

- GOTCHA: San Benito has only two incorporated cities (Hollister, San Juan Bautista). The Hollister Fire Department is the regional structural fire AHJ for the city, San Juan Bautista, AND the unincorporated county under contract — but it is NOT the CUPA (hazmat is county Environmental Health).
- The county spans water board regions 3 and 5 (mostly Region 3, small portions Region 5) — confirm watershed for the controlling RWQCB.
