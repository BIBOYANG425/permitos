> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Placerville** (El Dorado County seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by El Dorado County Environmental Management Department (EMD), the countywide CUPA. https://www.eldoradocounty.ca.gov/Public-Safety-Justice/Waste-Disposal/Hazardous-Materials-Program/CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Placerville does not run its own fire department; fire protection and emergency medical services are provided under contract by the El Dorado County Fire Protection District (independent special district). https://www.cityofplacerville.org/ElDoradoCountyFireDepartment

## Building authority

- City of Placerville Building Division (within Community Development / Public Works). https://www.cityofplacerville.org/BuildingDivision

## Local code amendments

- Placerville adopts the California Building Standards Code (Title 24) with local amendments; confirm the adopted edition (statewide 2025 Title 24 took effect 2026-01-01). The El Dorado County FPD presented findings of fact for the 2025 California Fire Code applicable in Placerville.
