> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Live Oak** (Sutter County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs here and where the city's adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Sutter County Environmental Health Division (Development Services Department), the countywide CalEPA-designated CUPA.
- https://www.sutter.gov/government/county-departments/development-services/environmental-health/cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Fire service is provided to the City of Live Oak by the **Sutter County Fire Department under contract** (the county department was formed in 1996 partly from the former Live Oak FD). — https://www.sutter.gov/government/county-departments/office-of-emergency-services/sutter-county-fire-department
- GOTCHA: this arrangement is in flux. Per reporting (appeal-democrat, Dec 2023 / 2024), the Live Oak City Council voted to submit a one-year notice to terminate its contracts with the Sutter County Sheriff's Office and Fire Department over cost concerns; a contract version ran through Dec 31, 2029 with the city paying ~85% of operational cost. **Verify the current fire-service provider on the live City of Live Oak Fire Services page before relying on this.** — https://www.liveoakcity.org/departments/fire-services

## Building authority

- City of Live Oak Building & Code Enforcement Department — reviews plans, issues building permits, and performs inspections within the city. — https://www.liveoakcity.org/departments/building-code-enforcement

## Notes

- Live Oak is the second incorporated city in Sutter County. It runs its **own building & code enforcement department** but is **not** its own CUPA (served by the Sutter County CUPA).
- GOTCHA: Live Oak does **not** run a municipal fire department; it contracts fire service from Sutter County Fire, and that contract was under a termination notice as of late 2023/2024 — the current provider should be re-verified.
