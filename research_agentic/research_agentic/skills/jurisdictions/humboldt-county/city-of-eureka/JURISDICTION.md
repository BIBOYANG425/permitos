> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Eureka** (county seat, largest city in Humboldt County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Humboldt County Division of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Program (countywide CUPA).
- https://humboldtgov.org/701/Certified-Unified-Program-Agency-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Humboldt Bay Fire** — Joint Powers Authority that consolidated the former City of Eureka Fire Department with Humboldt No. 1 Fire Protection District (JPA signed 2012, crews fully integrated 2013-01-01). Serves the City of Eureka and greater Eureka area. — https://www.hbfire.org/
- (Fire prevention / fire-code AHJ for the city runs through Humboldt Bay Fire.)

## Building authority

- City of Eureka Development Services Department, Building Division (permits via OpenGov) — https://www.eurekaca.gov/164/Building

## Local code amendments

- City of Eureka was introducing ordinances to adopt the 2025 California Building and Fire Codes. Confirm the adopted Title 24/fire-code edition on the live city page. — https://www.eurekaca.gov/164/Building (exact Eureka code-adoption ordinance URL: UNVERIFIED)

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Eureka no longer has a standalone municipal fire department — it is **Humboldt Bay Fire** (JPA with Humboldt No. 1 FPD).
- Eureka runs its own Building Division but does NOT run its own CUPA (county CUPA serves it).
