> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Fortuna** (Humboldt County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Humboldt County Division of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Program (countywide CUPA).
- https://humboldtgov.org/701/Certified-Unified-Program-Agency-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Fortuna Fire Protection District** (Fortuna Volunteer Fire Department) — serves the City of Fortuna plus outlying Alton, Hydesville, Metropolitan, Fernbridge, and Palmer Creek; mostly volunteer with a small career staff (Fire Chief, Career Division Chief, Fire Prevention Officer). — https://fortunafire.com/

## Building authority

- City of Fortuna Community Development Department, Building Division — https://www.friendlyfortuna.com/departments/community_development/building_division.php

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Fortuna's fire AHJ is the **Fortuna Fire Protection District** (Fortuna Volunteer Fire Department), a largely volunteer agency, not a full-time city fire department.
- Fortuna does NOT run its own CUPA (county CUPA serves it).
