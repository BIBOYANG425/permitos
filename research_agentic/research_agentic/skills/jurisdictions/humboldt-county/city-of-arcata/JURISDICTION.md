> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Arcata** (Humboldt County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Humboldt County Division of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Program (countywide CUPA).
- https://humboldtgov.org/701/Certified-Unified-Program-Agency-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Arcata Fire Protection District** — independent fire protection district (not a city department) serving the City of Arcata plus unincorporated Bayside, Manila, McKinleyville, and Jacoby Creek. Fire Marshal does fire/life-safety plan review in conjunction with the city/county building departments. — https://www.arcatafire.org/

## Building authority

- City of Arcata Community Development Department, Building Division — https://www.cityofarcata.org/163/Building-Division

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Arcata does NOT run a municipal fire department — fire AHJ is the independent **Arcata Fire Protection District**.
- Arcata does NOT run its own CUPA (county CUPA serves it).
