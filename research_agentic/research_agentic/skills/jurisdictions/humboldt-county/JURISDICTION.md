> Local EHS authority reference for **Humboldt County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Humboldt County Division of Environmental Health, Hazardous Materials Program — sole CUPA for the **entire county including all incorporated cities** (no city runs its own CUPA). Administers HMBP/Business Plan, hazardous waste generator + onsite treatment, UST, APSA, and CalARP.
- https://humboldtgov.org/701/Certified-Unified-Program-Agency-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- No countywide county fire department. Unincorporated areas are served by a patchwork of independent fire protection districts, community services districts, volunteer fire companies, Tribes, and JPAs; areas outside any district receive volunteer/"goodwill" coverage. State responsibility/wildland lands fall under CAL FIRE Humboldt-Del Norte Unit (HQ Fortuna). — https://humboldtgov.org/698/Fire-Protection-Services
- CAL FIRE Humboldt-Del Norte Unit — https://www.fire.ca.gov/

## Building authority

- Humboldt County Planning & Building Department, Building Inspection Division — jurisdiction over **unincorporated areas only** (explicitly excludes the cities of Eureka, Arcata, Trinidad, Rio Dell, Fortuna, Ferndale, and Blue Lake) — https://humboldtgov.org/153/Building-Inspection

## Local code amendments

- Humboldt County Code Title III, Chapter 1 (§ 331-11, Incorporation of Uniform Codes by Reference) adopts the California Building Standards Code by reference. Confirm the adopted Title 24 edition on the live page (county code text historically referenced an older edition while the statewide 2025 edition took effect 2026-01-01). — https://humboldt.county.codes/Code/331-11

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **The county CUPA covers every incorporated city** — none of Humboldt's seven cities (Eureka, Arcata, Fortuna, Ferndale, Rio Dell, Blue Lake, Trinidad) runs its own CUPA or is a separate Participating Agency.
- GOTCHA: **No unified county fire department.** Fire AHJ varies block-to-block by district; the City of Eureka FD was dissolved into the **Humboldt Bay Fire** JPA (with Humboldt No. 1 FPD), fully integrated 2013-01-01.
- The largest cities each operate their OWN building department; only the unincorporated county is served by the County Building Inspection Division.
