> Local EHS authority reference for **Yuba County**. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Yuba County Environmental Health Division (within the Community Development & Services Agency) — the **single CUPA for the entire county including the incorporated cities of Marysville and Wheatland** (HMBP/HMRRP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA/aboveground tanks, CalARP). No city in Yuba County runs its own CUPA. Located at 915 8th Street, Suite 123, Marysville, CA 95901; (530) 749-5450.
- https://www.yuba.gov/departments/community_development/environmental_health/hazardous_materials_(cupa)/index.php
- CalEPA CERS Unified Program Regulator Directory (select "Yuba" county) — "Yuba County Environmental Health Department" is the listed UPA: https://cersapps.calepa.ca.gov/public/directory/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- CAL FIRE Nevada-Yuba-Placer Unit (NEU) — provides fire protection and emergency medical services to the **unincorporated areas** of Yuba County (along with Nevada, Placer, Sierra and Sutter counties) — https://www.yuba.gov/departments/emergency_services/fire.php and https://www.fire.ca.gov/
- Numerous local fire protection districts / volunteer departments cover unincorporated communities (e.g. Linda FPD, Olivehurst, Smartsville FPD, Dobbins/Oregon House FPD, Foothill VFD) — verify the specific district for a given parcel.
- City of Marysville and the City of Wheatland have their own arrangements — see the city files.

## Building authority

- Yuba County Building Department (Community Development & Services Agency, Building Division) — application intake, plan-check, permit issuance and inspection for all buildings in the **unincorporated area**; also provides building services to the City of Wheatland by contract. 915 8th Street, Suite 123, Marysville, CA 95901; (530) 749-5440 — https://www.yuba.gov/departments/community_development/building_department/index.php

## Local code amendments

- Yuba County Code of Ordinances, Title X (Building Code) — Chapter 10.05 "Building, Structures, Construction Codes" adopts the California Building Standards Code, Title 24. The county Building Department states plans submitted on or after 2026-01-01 must comply with the **2025** California Administrative Code and 2025 California Building Code (Title 24) — confirm on the live page — https://library.municode.com/ca/yuba_county/codes/code_of_ordinances?nodeId=TITXBUCO_CH10.05BUSTCOCO

## Notes

- GOTCHA (federal-land carve-out): **Beale Air Force Base** lies within Yuba County and is served by its own DoD fire department; the base is federal land outside county AHJ/CUPA jurisdiction for most purposes — confirm program-by-program.
- GOTCHA: The City of **Marysville** (county seat) runs its own independent municipal fire department, and its Marysville hazmat response vehicle serves Yuba and Sutter counties — but Marysville is still served by the **county CUPA** (it does not run its own CUPA). See city-of-marysville.
- GOTCHA: The City of **Wheatland** is served by the **Wheatland Fire Authority**, a Joint Powers Agency (JPA) formed in 2005 by the City of Wheatland and the Plumas Brophy Fire Protection District — not by the county or CAL FIRE directly. Wheatland contracts building permitting/inspection to the **county**. See city-of-wheatland.
- Do not confuse with the **City of Yuba City**, which is in neighboring **Sutter County** (not Yuba County).
