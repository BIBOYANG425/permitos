> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Wheatland** (Yuba County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs inside the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Yuba County Environmental Health Division (Community Development & Services Agency), the countywide CUPA — https://www.yuba.gov/departments/community_development/environmental_health/hazardous_materials_(cupa)/index.php

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Wheatland Fire Authority — a **Joint Powers Agency (JPA)** formed in 2005 by the City of Wheatland and the Plumas Brophy Fire Protection District; provides fire and EMS to the city and the Plumas Brophy district — https://www.wheatlandfireauthority.org/

## Building authority

- **Contracted to Yuba County** — the City of Wheatland contracts building permitting and inspection services to the Yuba County Building Department (915 8th Street, Suite 123, Marysville; (530) 749-5440) — https://www.yuba.gov/departments/community_development/building_department/index.php

## Notes

- GOTCHA: Wheatland's fire AHJ is the **Wheatland Fire Authority JPA** (city + Plumas Brophy FPD), not the county or CAL FIRE.
- GOTCHA: Wheatland does not run its own building department — building plan-check and inspection are performed by **Yuba County** under contract.
