> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Chowchilla** (Madera County's second incorporated city). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local EHS programs within the city and where its adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Madera County Environmental Health Division (CalEPA-designated CUPA, countywide). https://www.maderacounty.com/government/community-economic-development-department/divisions/environmental-health-division/certified-unified-program-agency-cupa

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Chowchilla Fire Department — municipal department (largely volunteer, with full-time Battalion Chief and Fire Inspector positions); AHJ within city limits. https://cityofchowchilla.org/168/Fire-Department

## Building authority

- City of Chowchilla Building Division (City Hall). https://www.cityofchowchilla.org/157/Building

## Notes

- Chowchilla runs its own fire and building functions but relies on the county Environmental Health Division for all CUPA/hazardous-materials regulation.
