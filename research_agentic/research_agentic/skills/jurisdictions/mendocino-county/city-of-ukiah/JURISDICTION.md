> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Ukiah** (county seat and largest city of Mendocino County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city limits and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **Mendocino County Division of Environmental Health (CUPA)**. — https://www.mendocinocounty.gov/departments/public-health/environmental-health/hazardous-materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Ukiah Valley Fire Authority (UVFA)** — Joint Powers Authority effective July 1, 2017 between the **City of Ukiah** and the **Ukiah Valley Fire Protection District**; provides fire, rescue, and EMS to the city and unincorporated Ukiah Valley. Fire Prevention (plan review, fire permits, inspections) is run through the city. — https://cityofukiah.com/uvfa/

## Building authority

- **City of Ukiah — Community Development Department, Building Services Division** (Building Inspection / Code Enforcement / Fire Prevention), Civic Center, 300 Seminary Ave. Issues building and fire permits and conducts inspections within the city limits. — https://cityofukiah.com/community-development/building-services/

## Local code amendments

- Title 24 adopted via the **Ukiah City Code, Division 3** (Building Regulations), published at codepublishing.com. Specific 2025-edition adopting ordinance UNVERIFIED. — https://www.codepublishing.com/CA/Ukiah/

## Notes

- GOTCHA (fire JPA): Ukiah's AHJ is the **Ukiah Valley Fire Authority** JPA, not a standalone "Ukiah Fire Department" — it jointly serves the city and the surrounding Ukiah Valley FPD.
- Ukiah runs its **own building department** (unlike Fort Bragg and Point Arena, which use the county).
