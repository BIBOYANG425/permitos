> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Fort Bragg** (coastal city in Mendocino County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city limits and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the **Mendocino County Division of Environmental Health (CUPA)**. — https://www.mendocinocounty.gov/departments/public-health/environmental-health/hazardous-materials

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **Fort Bragg Fire Department** — operated under a Joint Powers Authority formed in 1990 between the **City of Fort Bragg** and the **Fort Bragg Rural Fire Protection District**; jointly provides fire service to the city and outlying rural areas. Predominantly volunteer. — https://www.city.fortbragg.com/services/fire_department/index.php

## Building authority

- **Not its own building department** — building permits, plan review, and inspections within the City of Fort Bragg are administered by the **Mendocino County Department of Planning & Building Services — Building Division** (which has a Fort Bragg office). — https://www.mendocinocounty.gov/departments/planning-building-services/building-division

## Notes

- GOTCHA (building handled by county): Although Fort Bragg is an incorporated city, its building/construction permitting is handled by the **county** Building Division, not by a city building department.
- GOTCHA (fire JPA): The AHJ is the **Fort Bragg Fire Department** JPA (City + Fort Bragg Rural FPD), not a county or CAL FIRE structural department.
