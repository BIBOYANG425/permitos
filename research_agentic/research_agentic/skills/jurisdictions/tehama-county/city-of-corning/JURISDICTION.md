> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Corning** (Tehama County). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Tehama County Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA). HMBP, hazardous waste generators, USTs, APSA, and CalARP for facilities in Corning are administered by the county.
- https://www.tehama.gov/government/departments/environmental-health/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Corning Volunteer Fire Department — single-station, all-volunteer department serving the City of Corning and surrounding area; the City of Corning operates the fire dispatch. 814 5th Street, Corning, CA 96021; phone (530) 824-7044 — https://www.corning.org/departments/fire-department/

## Building authority

- City of Corning building/permitting (Community Development) — building permits within the city limits. Verify the live department page for the current adopted Title 24 edition — https://www.corning.org/

## Notes

- The City of Corning runs its OWN volunteer fire department (not Tehama County Fire / CAL FIRE), but does NOT run its own CUPA — hazardous materials programs are handled by Tehama County Environmental Health.
