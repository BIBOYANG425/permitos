> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Red Bluff** (Tehama County seat). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs within the city and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Tehama County Environmental Health Division (the countywide CUPA). HMBP, hazardous waste generators, USTs, APSA, and CalARP for facilities in Red Bluff are administered by the county.
- https://www.tehama.gov/government/departments/environmental-health/

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Red Bluff Fire Department — independent municipal department (combination career/reserve), 555 Washington Street, Red Bluff, CA 96080. Holds automatic/mutual aid agreements with CAL FIRE Tehama-Glenn Unit and Tehama County Fire — https://www.rbfd.org/

## Building authority

- City of Red Bluff Community Development Department, Building Division — handles building permits and inspections within the city limits — https://www.cityofredbluff.org/departments/community_development/building_permit.php

## Notes

- The City of Red Bluff runs its OWN municipal fire department (not Tehama County Fire / CAL FIRE), but does NOT run its own CUPA — hazardous materials programs are handled by Tehama County Environmental Health.
