## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the San Joaquin County Environmental Health Department (countywide CUPA). HMBP/CalARP/UST/APSA/hazwaste-generator submissions go through county EHD via CERS. — https://www.sjgov.org/department/envhealth/programs/certified-unified-program-agency-(cupa)

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- **South San Joaquin County Fire Authority (SSJCFA / "South County Fire")** — a joint authority formed in September 1999 by the merger of the City of Tracy Fire Department and the Tracy Rural Fire Protection District. It provides fire protection for the City of Tracy plus the Tracy Rural Fire District (unincorporated Banta, Carbona, Lammersville, Vernalis). — https://www.cityoftracy.org/our-city/departments/fire-department

## Building authority

- City of Tracy Community & Economic Development Department — Building Safety Division, 333 Civic Center Plaza, (209) 831-6400. — https://www.cityoftracy.org/our-city/departments/building-safety
