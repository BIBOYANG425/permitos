> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Manteca** (San Joaquin County's 3rd-largest city, ~84k). Orientation for the research subagent: who administers local programs and where adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the San Joaquin County Environmental Health Department (countywide CUPA). HMBP/CalARP/UST/APSA/hazwaste-generator submissions go through county EHD via CERS. — https://www.sjgov.org/department/envhealth/programs/certified-unified-program-agency-(cupa)

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Manteca Fire Department (full municipal department; provides fire protection, EMS, hazmat response, and prevention). Fire Prevention Division handles code/permit functions. — https://www.manteca.gov/departments/fire-department

## Building authority

- City of Manteca Development Services Department — Building Safety Division (the code's "enforcing agency"), 1215 W. Center St., Suite 201; Building Safety (209) 456-8550. — https://www.manteca.gov/departments/development-services/building-safety/building-permits

## Notes

- Note the city limits of Manteca (municipal fire dept) vs. the surrounding **Lathrop-Manteca Fire Protection District**, which covers the City of Lathrop and unincorporated/rural Manteca and Lathrop areas — different AHJ from the City of Manteca FD. — https://www.lmfire.org/suppression/page/regional-resources
- Manteca building/fire code amendments are in the Manteca Municipal Code (eCode360, Ch. 15.04 Building Code). Confirm the adopted CBC edition (2022 vs 2025) on the live code page. — https://ecode360.com/44089437
