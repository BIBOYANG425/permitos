## EHS Local Rules by Family

### Air (SCAQMD)
- **South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD)** controls all air permits in LA
- Permit applications: https://www.aqmd.gov/home/permits
- ePermits portal: https://webpub.aqmd.gov
- Key rules: Rule 201/202 (permit to construct/operate), Rule 219 (exemptions), Rule 222 (registration)
- SCAQMD: (909) 396-2000

### Hazardous Materials / CUPA
- **LA County Fire Department, Health Hazardous Materials Division (HHMD)** is the CUPA for the City of Los Angeles
- NOTE: LAFD and LA County Fire are different agencies — for City of LA industrial areas, LA County Fire is the CUPA despite city incorporation
- CUPA contact: (323) 881-2300
- CERS: https://cers.calepa.ca.gov
- HMBP inspections on ~3-year cycle for most facilities

### Wastewater / Industrial Pretreatment
- **LA Sanitation (LASAN)** Pretreatment Section controls industrial discharges to the City sewer system
- Industrial Waste Permit required for significant industrial users
- Apply: https://www.lacitysan.org/san/faces/home/portal/s-lsh-es/s-lsh-es-iw
- Contact: (800) 773-2489
- Parameters regulated: metals (Cd, Cr, Cu, Pb, Ni, Zn), cyanide, TSS, BOD, O&G, pH
- Annual permit with sampling/reporting requirements

### Stormwater
- City of LA stormwater permit: Low Impact Development ordinance
- Industrial facilities in LA must comply with State IGP
- LADOT/LASAN stormwater division for local compliance support

### Fire Code / Hazmat Operational Permits
- **Los Angeles Fire Department (LAFD)** is the AHJ
- LAFD Fire Prevention & Public Safety Division: http://lafd.org/fire-prevention
- Hazmat permits: LAFD Hazardous Materials Section, (213) 485-6177
- Los Angeles has adopted CFC with LA Fire Code amendments (LAFC)
- High-piled storage permits: LAFD, Fire Prevention Bureau

### Zoning / Land Use
- Los Angeles Department of City Planning (LADCP)
- LA Municipal Code, Title 1, Chapter 1, Article 2 (Zoning)
- Industrial zones: M1 (Limited Industrial), M2 (Light Industrial), M3 (Heavy Industrial)
- CUP through LADCP: https://www.planning.lacity.org
- Planning counter: (213) 482-7077
