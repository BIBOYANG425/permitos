## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by Nevada County Environmental Health Department (the countywide CUPA). HMBP/CERS, hazardous waste generator, UST, and APSA programs for businesses in Grass Valley are administered by the county.
- https://www.nevadacountyca.gov/2144/Hazardous-Materials-CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- City of Grass Valley Fire Department (GVFD) — a municipal fire department. Following a 2018 shared-administration pilot and a 2020 full administrative merger, GVFD also provides Nevada City's fire services by contract (single fire chief, shared staffing across Grass Valley Stations 1 & 2 and Nevada City Station 5). GVFD, Nevada City, and the Nevada County Consolidated Fire District operate under a long-standing Joint Operational Area (JOA) (boundary-drop / closest-resource response). https://www.grassvalleyca.gov/fire-department

## Building authority

- City of Grass Valley Building Department (the City runs its own building department; permits via the City's online portal). https://www.grassvalleyca.gov/building (specific permit-portal vendor and direct phone UNVERIFIED)

## Local code amendments

- City of Grass Valley requires all new plan review and permit applications on or after 2026-01-01 to comply with the 2025 California Building Standards Codes (Building, Electrical, Mechanical, Plumbing, Energy, and Fire). Building & construction provisions are in Municipal Code Title 15 (Municode). City code page: https://www.grassvalleyca.gov/building-code-updates — Municode Title 15: https://library.municode.com/ca/grass_valley/codes/code_of_ordinances?nodeId=TIT15BUCO

## Notes

- Runs its own municipal fire department and its own building department, but EHS/CUPA is the county's (verified).
- Fire-dept merger (gotcha): GVFD absorbed Nevada City's fire administration via a 2018 pilot and 2020 full merger; Nevada City now contracts fire services through Grass Valley FD. The two cities and the Nevada County Consolidated Fire District also run a long-standing JOA with shared station staffing (verified via 2020 merger reporting).
