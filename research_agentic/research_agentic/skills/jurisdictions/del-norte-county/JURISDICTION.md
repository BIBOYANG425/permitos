## CUPA (Unified Program)

- Del Norte County Environmental Health Division — the **sole countywide CUPA** (HMBP, hazardous waste generator, UST, APSA, CalARP). No city in the county runs its own CUPA or is a separate Participating Agency; Crescent City is covered by the County CUPA. Submissions go through CERS.
- https://www.co.del-norte.ca.us/departments/EnvironmentalHealth/CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Unincorporated / State Responsibility Area: CAL FIRE Humboldt–Del Norte Unit — https://www.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-protection/cal-fire-units (confirm unit page live)
- Crescent City Fire & Rescue (volunteer) serves the City of Crescent City and surrounding district — https://www.crescentcity.org/departments/Fire
- Crescent Fire Protection District — https://cfpd.crescentcity.org/

## Building authority

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **Crescent City is the only incorporated city in Del Norte County** (2020 pop. ~6,000; county ~27,700). All other communities are unincorporated.
- GOTCHA: **Fire consolidation** — Crescent City Fire & Rescue is the product of a 2015 operational merger by agreement between the City of Crescent City and the Crescent Fire Protection District; it is an all-volunteer department. The City does not staff a traditional paid municipal fire department.
- The Del Norte County CUPA Director also serves as Agricultural Commissioner, Sealer of Weights & Measures, and Air Pollution Control Officer (small rural agency consolidation). Air district handled separately and out of scope here.
