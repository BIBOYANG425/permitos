> Local EHS authority reference for the **City of Crescent City**, the county seat and only incorporated city in Del Norte County. Orientation for the research subagent: who administers the local programs and where their adopted codes live. Verify the live page before quoting; the 2025 California Building Standards Code (Title 24) replaced the 2022 edition statewide on 2026-01-01, so confirm the adopted edition.

## CUPA (Unified Program)

- **Not its own CUPA** — served by the Del Norte County Environmental Health Division (countywide CUPA).
- https://www.co.del-norte.ca.us/departments/EnvironmentalHealth/CUPA

## Fire authority (AHJ)

- Crescent City Fire & Rescue (volunteer) — product of a 2015 operational merger by agreement between the City of Crescent City and the Crescent Fire Protection District. The City owns one station; the District owns three.
- https://www.crescentcity.org/departments/Fire

## Building authority

- Crescent City Building Department (377 J Street, Crescent City) — issues building permits within city limits.
- https://www.crescentcity.org/departments/Building

## Notes

- GOTCHA: **Fire is shared, not independently city-staffed** — Crescent City Fire & Rescue operates jointly with the Crescent Fire Protection District under the 2015 merger agreement, so the AHJ spans both city and adjacent district territory.
