## Triggers

- Facility generates a waste that may meet California-only hazardous criteria.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| California hazardous waste | Waste meets STLC/TTLC criteria in 22 CCR 66261.24 even if not federally toxic | 22 CCR 66261.24 / 66261.101 |

## Exemptions

- A waste is presumed RCRA unless shown non-RCRA per 22 CCR 66261.101. California criteria are broader than federal RCRA.
