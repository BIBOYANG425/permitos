## Manifest Requirement
ALL off-site shipments of hazardous waste in California require a **Uniform Hazardous Waste Manifest** (EPA Form 8700-22) regardless of quantity.

## California e-Manifest (EPA e-Manifest System)
- Portal: https://rcrainfo.epa.gov/rcrainfoprod/action/secured/login
- California participates in the federal e-Manifest system
- Paper manifests still allowed but e-manifest recommended

## Required on Manifest
1. Facility EPA ID number (obtain from DTSC: https://www.dtsc.ca.gov/)
2. Generator name, address, EPA ID
3. Transporter name(s) and EPA ID(s)
4. Designated TSD facility name and EPA ID
5. Waste descriptions (DOT proper shipping name, UN/NA number, hazard class)
6. Quantities and containers
7. Generator certification signature

## California-Specific: Consolidated Manifest
Generators may use a consolidated manifest for multiple facilities IF all facilities are owned/operated by same entity — requires DTSC approval.

## Transporter Requirements
- Must have CA registered hazardous waste transporter registration (DTSC Form 1162)
- Registration at: https://www.dtsc.ca.gov/HazardousWaste/Transporters/
- Federal DOT requirements also apply (49 CFR Parts 171-180)
