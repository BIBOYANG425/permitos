## Triggers

- A facility whose SIC code is among those regulated by the Industrial General Permit.
- Industrial activity exposed to stormwater (materials handling, equipment, or processes exposed to rain/runoff).

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| IGP coverage required | Facility's SIC/NAICS is on the regulated list AND no exclusion applies | California Industrial General Permit |

## Exemptions

- Some facilities qualify for a No Exposure Certification (NEC) or another exclusion — verify against the fetched permit whether the facility's activities are actually exposed to stormwater before treating IGP as not applicable.
