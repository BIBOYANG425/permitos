## SMARTS Portal
**SMARTS** (Stormwater Multiple Application Reporting and Tracking System): https://smarts.waterboards.ca.gov
All IGP reporting must go through SMARTS — the NOI, annual reports, sampling results, and exceedance reports.

## Monitoring Program (IGP Section X)
| Requirement | Frequency | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Visual observations | Each qualifying rain event (QRE) | Minimum 2/year wet season |
| Sampling (Baseline) | 2 QREs/year minimum | Benchmark comparison |
| Sampling (Level 1 Exceedance) | 4 QREs/year | Required if benchmark exceeded |
| Sampling (Level 2 Exceedance) | Monthly (wet season) + corrective action | Required if Level 1 insufficient |

## Benchmark Values (common parameters)
| Parameter | Benchmark |
|---|---|
| Total Suspended Solids (TSS) | 100 mg/L |
| pH | 6.0 – 9.0 |
| O&G (Oil and Grease) | 15 mg/L |
| COD | Varies by SIC code |

## Annual Report Due Date
**July 1** each year in SMARTS — covers the prior wet season (October 1–May 30).

## Exceedance Response Actions
- **Level 1**: Two consecutive samples above benchmark → implement Level 1 BMPs, report in SMARTS within 30 days
- **Level 2**: Two consecutive Level 1 samples still exceeding → implement corrective action plan (CAP) within 30 days; monthly monitoring; SMARTS notification
