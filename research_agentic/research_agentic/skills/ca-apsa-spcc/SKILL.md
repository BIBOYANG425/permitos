## Triggers

- Facility stores petroleum in aboveground containers, or is subject to the federal SPCC rule.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| APSA applies | Aggregate aboveground petroleum capacity >= 1,320 gallons, counting containers >= 55 gallons | HSC 25270 et seq.; 40 CFR 112 |

## Exemptions

- Containers < 55 gallons are not counted toward the aggregate. Tanks in an underground area (TIUGA) excluded per HSC 25270.3.
