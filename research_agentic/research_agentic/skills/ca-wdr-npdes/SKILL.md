The agent must still fetch the cited primary source and quote it verbatim: the skill ORIENTS, the fetched regulation is the PROOF. Never cite a SKILL.md as evidence.

## Triggers

- Facility discharges process wastewater to surface water or land, not covered by the IGP or POTW pretreatment.

## Thresholds

| What to look for | Expected orientation | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| WDRs / individual NPDES | Discharge of waste to waters of the state/US | Water Code 13260 et seq. (Porter-Cologne); CWA NPDES |

## Exemptions

- Discharges fully covered by the Industrial General Permit or a POTW pretreatment program are addressed there instead.
