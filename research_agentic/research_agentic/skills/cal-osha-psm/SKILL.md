> ORIENTATION ONLY. Thresholds orient the search; they are not evidence. Fetch and quote 8 CCR 5189 (and its Appendix A list of highly hazardous chemicals with threshold quantities) verbatim. Never cite a SKILL.md.

## Triggers

- A process uses, stores, manufactures, handles, or moves a listed highly hazardous chemical (8 CCR 5189 Appendix A) at or above its threshold quantity.
- A process involves 10,000 pounds or more of a flammable liquid or flammable gas on site (with limited exceptions).

## Thresholds

| Material | Expected threshold (verify in Appendix A) | Primary source to fetch and quote |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Listed highly hazardous chemical | chemical-specific threshold quantity | 8 CCR 5189, Appendix A |
| Flammable liquid or gas (process) | 10,000 lb | 8 CCR 5189(a) |

## Exemptions

- Retail facilities, oil/gas well activities, and normally unoccupied remote facilities have specific exclusions — confirm in the fetched standard.
- Flammable liquids stored in atmospheric tanks / used as fuel may be excluded — verify the exact exclusion language.
- Below-threshold quantities do not trigger PSM, but may still fall under the federal/Cal-EPA accidental-release programs ([[ca-calarp-program]]).
